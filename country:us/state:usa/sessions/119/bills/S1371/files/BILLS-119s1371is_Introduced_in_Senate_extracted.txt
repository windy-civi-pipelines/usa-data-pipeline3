Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to increase the number of eligible shareholders of an S corporation.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the S-Corporation Additional Participation Act of 2025 or the S-CAP Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Increase in number of shareholders to qualify as an S corporation(a)In generalSection 1361(b)(1)(A) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking 100 and inserting 250.(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
