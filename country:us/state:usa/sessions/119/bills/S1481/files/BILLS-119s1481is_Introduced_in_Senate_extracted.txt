Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to reinstate advance refunding bonds.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Lifting Our Communities through Advance Liquidity for Infrastructure Act or the LOCAL Infrastructure Act.

Section 2:
2.Reinstatement of advance refunding bonds(a)In generalThe amendments made by section 13532 of Public Law 115–97 are repealed and the provisions of law amended by such section are restored as if such section had never been enacted.(b)Effective dateThe repeal made by this section shall take effect on the date of enactment of this Act.
