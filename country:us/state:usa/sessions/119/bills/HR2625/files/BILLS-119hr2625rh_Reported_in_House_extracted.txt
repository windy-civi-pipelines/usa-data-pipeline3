Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 38, United States Code, to update certain terminology regarding veteran employment.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Reported in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Veterans Employment Readiness Yield Act of 2025 or the VERY Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Update of certain terminology regarding veteran employmentTitle 38, United States Code, is amended—(1)by striking employment handicap each place it occurs and inserting employment barrier; and(2)by striking employment handicaps each place it occurs and inserting employment barriers.
