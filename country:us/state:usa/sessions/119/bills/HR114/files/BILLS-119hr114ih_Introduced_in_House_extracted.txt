Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Responsible Path to Full Obamacare Repeal Act.

Section 2:
2. Repeal of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act and the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010(a)Patient Protection and Affordable Care ActEffective October 1, 2025, the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (Public Law 111–148) is repealed, and the provisions of law amended or repealed by such Act are restored or revived as if such Act had not been enacted.(b)Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010Effective October 1, 2025, the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010 (Public Law 111–152) is repealed, and the provisions of law amended or repealed by such Act are restored or revived as if such Act had not been enacted.
