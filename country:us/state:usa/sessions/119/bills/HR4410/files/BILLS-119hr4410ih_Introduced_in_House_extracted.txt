Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Department of State Authorization Act of 2023 to
            extend the special hiring authority for passport services.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Cutting Passport Backlog Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Extension of special hiring authority for passport services
 Section 6101 of the Department of State Authorization Act of 2023 (22 U.S.C. 211a note) is amended by striking 3-year period and inserting 5-year period.
