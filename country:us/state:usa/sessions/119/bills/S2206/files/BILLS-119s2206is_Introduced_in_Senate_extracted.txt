Section 1:
1.Increase in limitation for qualified higher education expenses from 529 accounts(a)In generalThe last sentence of section 529(e)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking $10,000 and inserting $20,000.(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
