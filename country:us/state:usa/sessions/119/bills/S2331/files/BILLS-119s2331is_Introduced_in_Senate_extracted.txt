Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act
            to include extreme temperature in the definition of a major disaster.
Number of Sections: 3
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Extreme Heat Emergency Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.
            Definition of major disaster
 Section 102(2) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (42 U.S.C. 5122(2)) is amended by inserting extreme temperature, before or drought.

Section 3:
3.
            No additional funds
 No additional funds are authorized to be appropriated for the purpose of carrying out this Act.

        A BILL

            2.
            Definition of major disaster
 Section 102(2) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (42 U.S.C. 5122(2)) is amended by inserting extreme temperature, before or drought.

 No additional funds are authorized to be appropriated for the purpose of carrying out this Act.
