Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 49, United States Code, to require air carriers to transfer funds to a ticket agent for cancelled or significantly delayed or changed flights within the time required for air carriers and ticket agents to provide refunds to passengers, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Flight Refund Fairness Act.

Section 2:
2.Refunds for cancelled or significantly delayed or changed flightsSection 42305(e) of title 49, United States Code, is amended—
(1)in paragraph (2), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking promptly and inserting , not later than the date described in subsection (b),; and (2)in paragraph (3)—
(A)by striking A refund and inserting the following:  (A)TimingIn providing refunds under this subsection, a ticket agent shall provide such a refund to an individual not later than 7 days after the ticket agent receives the funds transferred by the air carrier or foreign air carrier.
(B)AlternativesA refund; and  (B)in subparagraph (B), as designated by paragraph (2), by striking subsections (b) and and inserting subsection.
