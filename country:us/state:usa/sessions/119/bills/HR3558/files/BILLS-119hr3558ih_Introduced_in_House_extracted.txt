Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 38, United States Code, to increase the authorization of appropriations for homeless veterans reintegration programs.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short TitleThis Act may be cited as the Veteran Jobs Training Act.

Section 2:
2.Increase of authorization of appropriations for homeless veterans reintegration programsSection 2021(i)(1) of title 38, United States Code, is amended—
(1)in subparagraph (G), by striking fiscal year 2024 and each fiscal year thereafter and inserting each of fiscal years 2024 and 2025; and (2)by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:

(H) $75,000,000 for fiscal year 2024 and each fiscal year thereafter..
