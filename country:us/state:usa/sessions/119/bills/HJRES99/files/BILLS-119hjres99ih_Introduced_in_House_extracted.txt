Title: 
Official Title: Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of the United States to provide that new States may be admitted to the United States upon a concurrence of two thirds of each house of Congress.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
That the following article is proposed as an amendment to the Constitution of the United States, which shall be valid to all intents and purposes as part of the Constitution when ratified by the legislatures of three-fourths of the several States:  —New States may be admitted by the Congress into this Union upon the Concurrence of two thirds of each House..

Section 2:
New States may be admitted by the Congress into this Union upon the Concurrence of two thirds of each House.
