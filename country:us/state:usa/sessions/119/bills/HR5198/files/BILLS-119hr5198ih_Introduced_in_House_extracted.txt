Title: 
Official Title: To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to maintain standards for qualification as a rural health clinic under the Medicare program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Rural Health Clinic Location Modernization Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Maintaining standards for qualification as a rural health clinic under the Medicare program (a)In generalSection 1861(aa)(2) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395x(aa)(2)) is amended, in the first sentence of the matter following subparagraph (K), by striking an urbanized area (as defined by the Bureau of the Census) and inserting an urban area (as defined by the Bureau of the Census) with a population of 50,000 or more. 
(b)Effective dateThe amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply beginning January 1, 2027.
