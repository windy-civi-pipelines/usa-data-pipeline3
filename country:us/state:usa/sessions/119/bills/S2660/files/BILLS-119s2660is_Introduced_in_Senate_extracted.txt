Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 49, United States Code, to protect the use of risk-based
            approaches by owners and operators of certain pipeline facilities, and for other
            purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Modern Risk Detection Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.
            Risk-based approaches
 Section 60102 of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
            
                
                    (u)
                    Risk-Based approaches
 The Secretary shall ensure that the standards prescribed under this chapter allow for the use, to the maximum extent practicable, of risk-based approaches, concepts, or principles in complying with those standards.
                
                .

        A BILL
        To amend title 49, United States Code, to protect the use of risk-based
            approaches by owners and operators of certain pipeline facilities, and for other
            purposes.
    
    
        
            1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Modern Risk Detection Act of 2025.
        
        
            2.
            Risk-based approaches
 Section 60102 of title 49, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
            
                
                    (u)
                    Risk-Based approaches
 The Secretary shall ensure that the standards prescribed under this chapter allow for the use, to the maximum extent practicable, of risk-based approaches, concepts, or principles in complying with those standards.
