Official Title: To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize funding for grants and contracts for primary care training and enhancement, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Train More Primary Care Doctors Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Primary care training and enhancementSection 747(c)(1) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 293k(c)(1)) is amended by striking $48,924,000 for each of fiscal years 2021 through 2025 and inserting $49,924,000 for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2030.
