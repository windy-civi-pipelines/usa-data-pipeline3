Title: 
Official Title: To require the Secretary of the Interior to reissue a final rule relating to gray wolves.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Pacific Northwest Gray Wolves Relief Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Reissuance of final rule relating to gray wolves (a)In generalNot later than 60 days after the date of the enactment of this section, the Secretary of the Interior shall reissue the final rule titled Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Removing the Gray Wolf (Canis lupus) From the List of Endangered and Threatened Wildlife (85 Fed. Reg. 69778; November 3, 2020). 
(b)ApplicationThe final rule reissued under subsection (a) shall apply only with respect to gray wolf populations in the States of Oregon and Washington.
