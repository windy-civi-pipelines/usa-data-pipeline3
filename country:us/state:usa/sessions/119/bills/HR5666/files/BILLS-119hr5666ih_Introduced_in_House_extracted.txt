Section 1:
1.Continued operation of Coast GuardIn the case of a lapse in appropriations for the Coast Guard, there are appropriated such amounts as may be necessary for the operation of the Coast Guard for the lesser of— (1)30 days; or
(2)the duration of the lapse in appropriation.
