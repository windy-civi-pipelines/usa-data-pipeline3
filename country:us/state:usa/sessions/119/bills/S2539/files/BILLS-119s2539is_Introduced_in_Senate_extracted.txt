Title: 
Official Title: To redesignate the Department of Defense Africa Center for Strategic Studies as the James M. Inhofe Africa Center for Strategic Studies.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Chairman James Mountain Inhofe Recognition Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Redesignation of Africa Center for Strategic Studies as James M. Inhofe Africa Center for Strategic Studies(a)Reference to regional centers for strategic studiesSection 342(b)(2)(D) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by striking Africa Center for Strategic Studies and inserting James M. Inhofe Africa Center for Strategic Studies.(b)ReferencesAny reference to the Department of Defense Africa Center for Strategic Studies in any law, regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States shall be deemed to be a reference to the James M. Inhofe Africa Center for Strategic Studies.
