Section 1:
1.Reaffirmation of trust statusLand taken into trust by the United States pursuant to the Act of June 18, 1934 (25 U.S.C. 5108), before the date of enactment of this Act for the benefit of an Indian Tribe that was federally recognized on the date that the land was taken into trust is reaffirmed as trust land.
