Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Make Entertainment Great Again Act of 2025 or the MEGA Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Designation of Donald J. Trump Center for the Performing Arts 
(a)DesignationThe John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts shall after the date of enactment of this Act be known and designated as the Donald J. Trump Center for the Performing Arts. (b)ReferenceAny reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts shall be considered to be a reference to the Donald J. Trump Center for the Performing Arts.
