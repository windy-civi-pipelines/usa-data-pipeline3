Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the National Education Association Charter Repeal Act.

Section 2:
2.Repeal of Federal charter to the National Education Association(a)In generalChapter 1511 of title 36, United States Code (which granted a Federal charter to the National Education Association), is repealed.(b)Clerical amendmentThe table of chapters for title 36, United States Code, is amended by striking the items relating to chapter 1511.
