Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Protecting Community Television Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Modifying the definition of franchise fee
 Section 622(g)(1) of the Communications Act of 1934 (47 U.S.C. 542(g)(1)) is amended—
            
                (1)
 by striking includes and inserting means; and
            
            
                (2)
 by inserting other monetary before assessment.
