Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 49, United States Code, to authorize appropriations for the Federal-State partnership for intercity passenger rail grant program, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Federal-State Partnership for Intercity Passenger Rail Reauthorization Act.

Section 2:
2.Authorization of appropriations for Federal-State partnership for intercity passenger rail grantsSection 24911(h) of title 49, United States Code, is amended to read as follows: 
 
(h)Authorization of appropriations 
(1)In generalThere is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary for use carrying out this section $7,500,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2027 through 2031, to remain available until expended. (2)Project management oversight expensesThe Secretary may use up to 2 percent of amounts appropriated under paragraph (1) in a fiscal year to carry out project management oversight with respect to any grant program under this title authorized to be carried out in such fiscal year..
