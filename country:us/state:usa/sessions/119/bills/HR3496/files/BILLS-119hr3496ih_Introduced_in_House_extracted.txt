Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Small Business Act for microloan eligibility for the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Northern Mariana Islands Small Business Access Act.

Section 2:
2.Inclusion of the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands in the microloan program(a)In generalSection 7(m)(7)(B) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 636(m)(7)(B)) is amended by inserting , the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands after Guam each place it appears.(b)Technical amendmentSection 7(m)(11)(C)(ii) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 636(m)(11)(C)(ii)) is amended by striking rural and all that follows through the end of the clause and inserting rural; and.
