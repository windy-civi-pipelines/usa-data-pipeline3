Title: 
Official Title: To amend the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 to reauthorize the National Flood Insurance Program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the NFIP Extension Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Extension of the National Flood Insurance Program(a)FinancingSection 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4016(a)) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2025.(b)Program expirationSection 1319 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4026) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2025.(c)Retroactive effective dateIf this Act is enacted after March 14, 2025, the amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall take effect as if enacted on March 14, 2025.
