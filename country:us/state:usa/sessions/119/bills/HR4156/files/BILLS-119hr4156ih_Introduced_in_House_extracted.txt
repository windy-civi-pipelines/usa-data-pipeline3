Title: 
Official Title: To establish a pilot program to establish a pre-approval or pre-qualification process for direct farm ownership loans under subtitle A of the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act in order to streamline the application process and provide greater certainty to borrowers.
Number of Sections: 3
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Support for Ownership and Investment in Land Act or the SOIL Act.

Section 2:
2.Farmer loan pilot projectsSubtitle D of the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act is amended by inserting after section 333D (7 U.S.C. 1983d) the following: 
 
333E.Prior approval or qualification authorization pilot program 
(a)In generalBeginning not later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this section, the Secretary shall carry out a pilot program to establish a pre-approval or pre-qualification process for direct farm ownership loans made under subtitle A in order to streamline the application process and provide greater certainty to borrowers. (b)Eligibility requirements (1)In generalNotwithstanding any other provision of this Act, the Secretary may establish alternative eligibility requirements for direct farm ownership loans under subtitle A that are processed through the pilot program. 
(2)LimitationApplicants satisfying the alternative eligibility requirements shall remain subject to all other provisions of subtitle A. (c)Loan assessmentsIn carrying out the pilot program, the Secretary shall consider streamlining the determinations under section 360(b). 
(d)ReportNot later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this section, and annually thereafter, the Secretary shall submit to the Committee on Agriculture of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry of the Senate a report examining the actions undertaken under, and the results of, the pilot program. (e)Termination of effectivenessThe authority provided by this section shall terminate effective September 30, 2030..
