Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the provision of certain additional burial benefits for individuals for whom an urn or plaque is furnished, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Ensuring Veterans’ Final Resting Place Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Department of Veterans Affairs provision of additional burial benefits when an urn or plaque is furnished(a)In generalSection 2306(h) of title 38, United States Code, is amended—(1)in paragraph (1)—(A)by striking In lieu of furnishing a headstone or marker under this section for and inserting In the case of; and (B)by striking paragraph (3) and inserting paragraph (2); (2)by striking paragraph (2); and(3)by redesignating paragraphs (3) through (5) as paragraphs (2) through (4), respectively.(b)ApplicabilityThe amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to an individual who dies on or after January 5, 2021.
