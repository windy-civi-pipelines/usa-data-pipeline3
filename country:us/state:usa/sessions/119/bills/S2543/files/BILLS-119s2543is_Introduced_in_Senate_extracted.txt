Title: 
Official Title: To direct the Secretary of Agriculture to submit a report on New World screwworm readiness and response.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Stop the Screwworms With Active Readiness and Mitigation Act of 2025 or the Stop the SWARM Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Report on New World screwworm readiness and responseNot later than 30 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Agriculture shall submit to Congress a report on the New World Screwworm domestic readiness and response initiative of the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, with a particular focus on—(1)(A)domestic readiness, including the construction of a domestic production facility in the event of a threat of a domestic outbreak; and(B)exploring partnerships with States and industry with respect to that construction and other domestic preparedness efforts;(2)sterile fly production technology and other eradication tools and technologies; and(3)the benefits of and barriers, including timelines and costs, to enhanced domestic, as compared to international, sterile fly production.
