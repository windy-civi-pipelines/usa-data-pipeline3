Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit participation of persons convicted of certain felonies in programs of the Small Business Administration, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Stop Funding Rioters Act.

Section 2:
2.Persons convicted of certain felonies ineligible for Small Business Administration assistanceNo person convicted of an offense relating to a misdemeanor or felony assault of a law enforcement officer, or convicted of a felony for actions during or in connection with a riot that resulted in the destruction of a small business concern (as defined in section 3 of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 632)), shall be eligible to receive any assistance from or through, or participate in, any program administered by the Small Business Administration.
