Section 1:
1.
            Acquisition, integration, and accessibility of data of Digital Coast
                program
 Section 4 of the Digital Coast Act (16 U.S.C. 1467) is amended—
            
                (1)
 in subsection (b)(1), by inserting and fully and freely available after readily accessible;
            
            
                (2)
 in subsection (d)(3)(D), by inserting , including data related to underground infrastructure and subsurface utilities after data; and
            
            
                (3)
 in subsection (g), by striking 2025 and inserting 2030.
