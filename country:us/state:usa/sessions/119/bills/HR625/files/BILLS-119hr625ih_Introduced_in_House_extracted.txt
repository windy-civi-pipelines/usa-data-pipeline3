Title: 
Official Title: To clarify where court may be held for certain district courts in Texas and California.
Number of Sections: 3
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Local Access to Courts Act or LACA.

Section 2:
2.Organization of Texas district courtsSection 124(b)(2) of title 28, United States Code, is amended, in the matter preceding paragraph (3), by inserting and College Station before the period at the end.

Section 3:
3.Organization of California district courtsSection 84(d) of title 28, United States Code, is amended by inserting and El Centro after at San Diego.
