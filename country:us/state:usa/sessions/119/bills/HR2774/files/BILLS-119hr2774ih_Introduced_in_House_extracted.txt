Title: 
Official Title: To amend section 5 of the Flood Control Act of 1941 to impose a deadline for initiating the repair or restoration of project under such section, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Safeguarding Our Levees Act.

Section 2:
2.Emergency response to natural disastersSection 5 of the Flood Control Act of 1941 (33 U.S.C. 701n(a)) is amended—
(1)in subsection (a) by adding at the end the following:  (6)DeadlineNot later than 180 days after receiving from a non-Federal sponsor of a project a request to repair or rehabilitate a flood control work described in paragraph (1), the Secretary shall initiate the repair or restoration of such flood control work.; and
(2)in subsection (d)(2) by inserting 25 percent of before the difference.
