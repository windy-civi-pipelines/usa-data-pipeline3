Title: 
Official Title: To provide for the payment of staff of the Bureau of Prisons.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Bureau of Prisons Earnings Now Act of 2025 or as the BOPEN Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Payment of staff of the Bureau of Prisons 
(a)In generalThere are hereby appropriated out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for any period during which interim or full-year appropriations for fiscal year 2026 or fiscal year 2027 are not in effect such sums are necessary for the payment of salaries and expenses of the Bureau of Prisons. (b)ExceptionSubsection (a) does not apply in the case of any officer of the Bureau of Prisons who is required to be appointed by the President with the advice and consent of the Senate.
