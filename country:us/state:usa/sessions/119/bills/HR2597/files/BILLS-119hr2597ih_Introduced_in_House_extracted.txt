Title: 
Official Title: To codify Executive Order 14249 (relating to protecting America’s bank account against fraud, waste, and abuse).
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Protecting American Taxpayers from Wasteful Spending Act.

Section 2:
2.Codification of Executive Order 14249, relating to protecting America’s bank account against fraud, waste, and abuse Executive Order 14249 (90 Fed. Reg. 14011; relating to protecting America’s bank account against fraud, waste, and abuse, signed March 25, 2025), shall have the force and effect of law.
