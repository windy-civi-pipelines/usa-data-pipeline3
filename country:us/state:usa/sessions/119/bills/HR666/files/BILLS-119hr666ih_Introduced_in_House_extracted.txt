Title: 
Official Title: To provide a definition of reasonable rate for noncontiguous domestic ocean trade, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Noncontiguous Shipping Reasonable Rate Act of 2024.

Section 2:
2.Noncontiguous domestic ocean tradeSection 13701(d) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking paragraph (1) and inserting the following new paragraph:

(1)In generalFor purposes of this section, a rate is reasonable if such rate is within 10 percent of a rate set by a comparable international ocean rate index recognized by the Federal Maritime Commission..
