Title: 
Official Title: To limit the authority to impose duties on articles of food.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the No Tariffs on Groceries Act.

Section 2:
2.Limitation on authority to impose duties on articles of food(a)In generalThe President may not impose duties proposed by the Presidential Administration on articles of food unless—(1)the President transmits to Congress a request to impose such duties; and(2)such request is subsequently and specifically approved by an Act of Congress.(b)ExceptionThe limitation in subsection (a) shall not apply with respect to existing tariff-rate quotas on articles of food.(c)Articles of food definedIn this section, the term articles of food means—(1)articles used for food or drink for man or other animals;(2)articles used for components of any articles described in paragraph (1); and(3)seeds, fertilizers, manures, and agro-chemicals.
