Section 1:
1.Sheriff Adrian Butch Anderson Post Office Building
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 298 Route 292 in Holmes, New York, shall be known and designated as the Sheriff Adrian Butch Anderson Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Sheriff Adrian Butch Anderson Post Office Building.
