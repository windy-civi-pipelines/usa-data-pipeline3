Official Title: To reserve to Congress the authority to establish or extend a national monument.

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Ending Presidential Overreach on Public Lands Act.

Section 2:
2.National monumentsSection 320301 of title 54, United States Code (commonly referred to as the Antiquities Act), is amended to read as follows:  
 
320301.National monumentsThe establishment or extension of a national monument may be undertaken only by express authorization of Congress. .
