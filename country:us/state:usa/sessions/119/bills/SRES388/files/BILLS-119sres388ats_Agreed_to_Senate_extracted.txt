Section 1:
That the Senate—(1)recognizes September 16, 2025, as National Voter Registration Day; and(2)encourages each voting-eligible citizen of the United States—(A)to register to vote;(B)to verify with the appropriate State or local election official that the name, address, and other personal information on record is current; and(C)to go to the polls on election day and vote if the voting-eligible citizen would like to do so.

RES. 388IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESSeptember 15, 2025Mr. McConnell (for himself and Mr. Padilla) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed toRESOLUTIONRecognizing September 16, 2025, as National Voter Registration Day.That the Senate—(1)recognizes September 16, 2025, as National Voter Registration Day; and(2)encourages each voting-eligible citizen of the United States—(A)to register to vote;(B)to verify with the appropriate State or local election official that the name, address, and other personal information on record is current; and(C)to go to the polls on election day and vote if the voting-eligible citizen would like to do so.
