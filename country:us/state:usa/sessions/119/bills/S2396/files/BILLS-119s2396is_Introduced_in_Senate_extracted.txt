Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Federal Crop Insurance Act to require certain membership on the Board of Directors of the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Farm Board Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Board of Directors compositionSection 505(a)(2)(G) of the Federal Crop Insurance Act (7 U.S.C. 1505(a)(2)(G)) is amended by striking at least one specialty crop producer. and inserting the following:at least—(i)1 specialty crop producer; and(ii)beginning with the Board of Directors serving on or after May 1, 2027, 1 producer that actively engages in livestock production and crop production of agricultural commodities..
