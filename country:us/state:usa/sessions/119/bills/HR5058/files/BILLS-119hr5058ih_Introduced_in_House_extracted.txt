Section 1:
1.Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 46164 Westlake Drive in Sterling, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building.

R. 5058IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESAugust 26, 2025Mr. Subramanyam (for himself, Mr. Wittman, Mrs. Kiggans of Virginia, Mr. Scott of Virginia, Ms. McClellan, Mr. McGuire, Mr. Cline, Mr. Vindman, and Mr. Beyer) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 46164 Westlake Drive in Sterling, Virginia, as the Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building. 
1.Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 46164 Westlake Drive in Sterling, Virginia, shall be known and designated as the Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Firefighter Trevor Brown Post Office Building.
