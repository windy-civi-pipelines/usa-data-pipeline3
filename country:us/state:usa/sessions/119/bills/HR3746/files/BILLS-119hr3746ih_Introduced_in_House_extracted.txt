Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 49, United States Code, to make modifications to the passenger facility charge program administered by the Federal Aviation Administration, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Rebuilding America’s Airport Infrastructure Act.

Section 2:
2.Passenger facility charge(a)In generalSection 40117 of title 49, United States Code, is amended—(1)in subsection (b)—(A)in paragraph (1) by striking of $1, $2, $3, $4, or $4.50 and inserting described in paragraph (4); (B)by striking paragraph (4) and inserting the following:(4)Passenger facility charge amountThe maximum amount of a passenger facility charge imposed under paragraph (1) shall be—(A)for a 1-year period beginning on January 1, 2027, $5.50;(B)for a 1-year period beginning on January 1, 2028, $6.50;(C)for a 1-year period beginning on January 1, 2029, $7.50; and(D)on and after January 1, 2030, $8.50 (adjusted annually for inflation). ; (C)in paragraph (6)—(i)by striking paragraphs (1) and (4) and inserting paragraph (1); and(ii)by striking or (4); and (D)in paragraph (7)(A)—(i)by striking , (4),; and(ii)by striking or (4); and(2)in subsection (d)—(A)in paragraph (2)(C) by striking the semicolon and inserting ; and;(B)in paragraph (3) by striking ; and and inserting a period; and(C)by striking paragraph (4).(b)Effective dateThe amendments made by this section shall apply with respect to passenger facility charges imposed on or after January 1, 2027.
