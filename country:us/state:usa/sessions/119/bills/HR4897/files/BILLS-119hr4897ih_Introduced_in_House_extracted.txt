Title: 
Official Title: To amend section 148 of title 23, United States Code, to ensure blue envelope programs are eligible for certain safety project funding, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Blue Envelope Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Highway safety improvement programSection 148(a)(11)(B)(ii) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by striking laws; and inserting laws, including a project to improve interactions between a motor vehicle operator who has difficulty communicating through speech and a law enforcement officer during a motor vehicle stop, commonly referred to as a blue envelope program;.
