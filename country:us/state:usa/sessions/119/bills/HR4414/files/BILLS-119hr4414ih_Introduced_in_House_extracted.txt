Title: 
Official Title: To include commercial weather services as a priority area for
            funding under the Agriculture and Food Research Initiative of the Department of
            Agriculture, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Satellite-Based Agricultural Data Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Inclusion of commercial weather as AFRI priority area
 Subsection (b)(2)(E)(iii) of the Competitive, Special, and Facilities Research Grant Act (7 U.S.C. 3157(b)(2)(E)(iii)) is amended by inserting , including through the use of data and tools available from commercial weather services after mitigation measures.
