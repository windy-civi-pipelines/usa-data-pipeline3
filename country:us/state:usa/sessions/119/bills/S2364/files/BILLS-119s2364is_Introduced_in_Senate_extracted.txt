Section 1:
1.Single, unique zip codes for certain communitiesNot later than 270 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code for each of the following communities:(1)Mills, Wyoming.(2)Star Valley Ranch, Wyoming.
