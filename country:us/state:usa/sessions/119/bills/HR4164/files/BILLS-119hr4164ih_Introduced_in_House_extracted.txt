Section 1:
1.Jim Kolbe Memorial Post Office 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 100 North Taylor Lane in Patagonia, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Jim Kolbe Memorial Post Office.. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Jim Kolbe Memorial Post Office..

R. 4164IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 26, 2025Mr. Ciscomani (for himself, Mr. Hamadeh of Arizona, Mr. Stanton, Ms. Ansari, and Mr. Schweikert) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformA BILLTo designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 100 North Taylor Lane in Patagonia, Arizona, as the Jim Kolbe Memorial Post Office. 
1.Jim Kolbe Memorial Post Office 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 100 North Taylor Lane in Patagonia, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Jim Kolbe Memorial Post Office.. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Jim Kolbe Memorial Post Office..
