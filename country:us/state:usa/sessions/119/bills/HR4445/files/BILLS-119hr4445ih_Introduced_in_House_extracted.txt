Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize a public health and bio-preparedness workforce loan repayment program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Public Health and Bio-Preparedness Workforce Loan Repayment Reauthorization Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Reauthorization of Public Health and Bio-Preparedness Workforce Loan Repayment ProgramSection 776(i) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 295f–1(i)) is amended by striking fiscal years 2023 through 2025 and inserting fiscal years 2026 through 2030.
