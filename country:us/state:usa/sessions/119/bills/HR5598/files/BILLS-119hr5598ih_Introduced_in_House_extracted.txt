Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Revitalizing Rural Communities Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Extension of, and increase in funding for, the Rural Economic Development Loan and Grant ProgramSection 313B of the Rural Electrification Act of 1936 (7 U.S.C. 940c–2) is amended by striking $10,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023 and inserting $12,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030.
