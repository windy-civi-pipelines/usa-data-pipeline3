Section 1:
1.Authority of Secretary of Veterans Affairs to enter into a lease for a Vet Center in Mankato, MinnesotaSubject to the availability of appropriations for such purpose, the Secretary of Veterans Affairs may obligate or expend not more than $1,400,000 during fiscal years 2025 through 2026 to enter into a lease for a Vet Center (as defined in section 1712A of title 38, United States Code) in Mankato, Minnesota.
