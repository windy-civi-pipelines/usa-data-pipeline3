1.Reference, etc(a)Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Preventing Abusive Routine Tax Nonsense Enabled by Rip-offs Shelters and Havens and Instead Promoting Simplicity Act or the PARTNERSHIPS Act.(b)ReferenceExcept as otherwise expressly provided, whenever in this Act, an amendment or repeal is expressed in terms of an amendment to, or repeal of, a section or other provision, the reference shall be considered to be made to a section or other provision of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.(c)Table of contentsThe table of contents of this Act is as follows:Sec. 1.
