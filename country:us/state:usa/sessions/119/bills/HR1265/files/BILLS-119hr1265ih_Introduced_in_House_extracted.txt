Section 1:
1.Wastewater infrastructure and trash-free waters grantsSection 302 of the Save Our Seas 2.0 Act (33 U.S.C. 4282) is amended—(1)in subsection (c)(1)—(A)by inserting States, before municipalities; and (B)by inserting a comma before or Indian Tribes; and(2)in subsection (d)(1), by inserting States, before units of local government.
