Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 5, United States Code, to provide that dependent children under the age of 26 are eligible for coverage under the Federal Employees Dental and Vision Insurance Program, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Fix FEDVIP Age Act.

Section 2:
2.FEDVIP coverage for dependent children under age 26Section 8901(5) of title 5, United States Code, is amended by striking 22 each place it appears and inserting 26.
