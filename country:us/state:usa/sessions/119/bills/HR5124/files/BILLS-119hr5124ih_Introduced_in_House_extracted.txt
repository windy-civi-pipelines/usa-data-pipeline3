Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as River’s Law.

Section 2:
2. Safety requirements for child care providers Section 658E(c)(2)(I) of the Child Care and Development Block Grant Act of 1990 (42 U.S.C. 9858c(c)(2)(I)) is amended—
      
        (1)
 in clause (i)(XI), by striking ; and and inserting a semicolon;
      
      
        (2)
 by redesignating clause (ii) as clause (iii); and   (3) by inserting after clause (i) the following:
        
          
            (ii)
 shall include—  (I) a prohibition on constructing, maintaining, or otherwise operating a pool on the premises of any facility where such services are provided; and
            
            
              (II)
 a requirement to install door and window alarms in any facility where such services are provided; and
            
          
          .
