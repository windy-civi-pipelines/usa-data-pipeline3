Official Title: To repeal the Inflation Reduction Act of 2022.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Inflation Reduction Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Repeal of Inflation Reduction Act of 2022(a)In generalPublic Law 117–169 (commonly referred to as the Inflation Reduction Act of 2022) is repealed.(b)RescissionsThe unobligated balances of any amounts made available under Public Law 117–169 are rescinded.
