Section 1:
1.Christopher Pendergast Post Office Building
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 47 Echo Avenue in Miller Place, New York, shall be known and designated as the Christopher Pendergast Post Office Building. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Christopher Pendergast Post Office Building.
