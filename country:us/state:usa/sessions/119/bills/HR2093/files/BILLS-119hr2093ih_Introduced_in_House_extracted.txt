Section 1:
1.National pollutant discharge elimination system (NPDES) terms(a)In generalSection 402(b)(1)(B) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 U.S.C. 1342(b)(1)(B)) is amended to read as follows:(B)are for fixed terms—(i)not exceeding 10 years, for a permit issued to a State or municipality; and(ii)not exceeding 5 years, for a permit issued to any person not described in clause (i); and.(b)Technical correctionsSection 402(l)(3) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 U.S.C. 1342(l)(3)) is amended—(1)in subparagraph (B)—(A)by striking section 402 and inserting this section; and(B)by striking federal and inserting Federal; and (2)in subparagraph (C)—(A)by striking Section and inserting section;(B)by striking 402(p)(6) and inserting subsection (p)(6);(C)by striking 402(l)(3)(A), and inserting subparagraph (A),; and(D)by striking 402(l)(3)(A). and inserting such subparagraph..
