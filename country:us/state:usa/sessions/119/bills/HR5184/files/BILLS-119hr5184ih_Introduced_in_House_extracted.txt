Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit the Secretary of Energy from enforcing energy efficiency standards applicable to manufactured housing, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Affordable Housing Over Mandating Efficiency Standards Act or the Affordable HOMES Act.

Section 2:
2.Repeal of authority to establish standards for manufactured housing (a)Repeal (1)In generalThe Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 (42 U.S.C. 17071) is amended by striking section 413. 
(2)Clerical amendmentThe table of contents in section 1(b) of the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 is amended by striking the item relating to section 413.  (b) No force or effect The final rule titled Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Manufactured Housing published by the Department of Energy in the Federal Register on May 31, 2022 (87 Fed. Reg. 32728), shall have no force or effect.
