Title: 
Official Title: Of inquiry requesting the President and directing the Secretary of the Treasury to transmit, respectively, certain documents to the House of Representatives relating to the Department of Government Efficiency’s access to the Treasury payment systems and confidential taxpayer information.
Number of Sections: 1
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
That the President of the United States is requested, and the Secretary of the Treasury is directed, to transmit, to the extent that such documents are in the possession of the President or the Secretary, to the House of Representatives, not later than 14 days after the date of the adoption of this resolution, copies of any document, record, audio recording, memorandum, call log, correspondence (electronic or otherwise), activity logs, audit trails, audit logs, written agreements, or other communication or any portion of any such communication, that refers or relates to the following:(1)Access to, or usage of, the Treasury payment systems by the Department of Government Efficiency (hereafter referred to as DOGE), Elon Musk, or any member of his team. (2)Access to, or usage of, confidential tax returns or return information (within the meaning of section 6103 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, and including, but not limited to, social security numbers, names, addresses, type of tax, tax year, and tax debt owed) by DOGE, Elon Musk, or any member of his team.(3)Screenshots taken of Treasury payment systems data or records by DOGE, Elon Musk, or any member of his team.
