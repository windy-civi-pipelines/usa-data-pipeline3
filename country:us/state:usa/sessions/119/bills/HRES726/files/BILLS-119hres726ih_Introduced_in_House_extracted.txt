IV119th CONGRESS1st SessionH. RES. 726IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESSeptember 16, 2025Mr. Morelle (for himself, Ms. Sewell, Mrs. Torres of California, and Ms. Johnson of Texas) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on House AdministrationRESOLUTIONSupporting the recognition of September 16, 2025, as National Voter Registration Day. 
Whereas September 16th would be an appropriate day to establish National Voter Registration Day: Now, therefore, be it That the House of Representatives— 
(1)supports the designation of National Voter Registration Day; and (2)encourages each voting-eligible citizen of the United States to— 

                    (A)
 register to vote in his or her respective jurisdiction;  (B)confirm with their appropriate election official that his or her name, address, and other personal identifying information on record is current; and 
(C)fulfill his or her civic duty by voting on election day if he or she would like to do so.
