Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Arms Export Control Act to increase the threshold triggering certain notification requirements with respect to licenses for the exportation of firearms.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Firearms Congressional Notification Modernization Act.

Section 2:
2.Modification to notice requirementSection 36 of the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2776) is amended— (1)in subsection (a), by inserting (or, in the case of a defense article that is a firearm controlled under category I of the United States Munitions List, $4,000,000 or more) after $1,000,000 or more each place it appears; and 
(2)in subsection (c), by striking $1,000,000 and inserting $4,000,000.
