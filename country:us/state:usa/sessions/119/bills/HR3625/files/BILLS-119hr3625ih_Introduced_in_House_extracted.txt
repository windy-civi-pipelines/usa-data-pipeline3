Section 1:
1.Prohibition on the allocation of funds to the National Endowment for Democracy 
(a)ProhibitionThe head of an agency may not allocate any funds to the National Endowment for Democracy. (b)Agency definedIn this section the term agency has the meaning given that term in section 551 of title 5, United States Code.
