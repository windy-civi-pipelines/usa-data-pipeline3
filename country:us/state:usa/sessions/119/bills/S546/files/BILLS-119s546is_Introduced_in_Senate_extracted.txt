Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009 to make a technical correction to the water rights settlement for the Shoshone-Paiute Tribes of the Duck Valley Reservation, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Technical Correction to the Shoshone-Paiute Tribes of the Duck Valley Reservation Water Rights Settlement Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Authorization of payment of adjusted interest on Development FundSection 10807(b)(3) of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009 (Public Law 111–11; 123 Stat. 1409) is amended—(1)by striking There is and inserting the following:(A)In generalThere is; and(2)by adding at the end the following:(B)Adjusted interest paymentsThere is authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary for deposit into the Development Fund $5,124,902.12..
