Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make permanent the deduction for qualified business income.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Main Street Tax Certainty Act.

Section 2:
2.Deduction for qualified business income made permanent(a)In generalSection 199A of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking subsection (i).(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by this section shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2025.
