Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2022 with respect to a civil action relating to the disclosure of intimate images.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Increased Accountability for Nonconsensual Pornography Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Civil action relating to disclosure of intimate imagesSection 1309 of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2022 (15 U.S.C. 6851) is amended— 
(1)in subsection (a)— (A)in paragraph (2), by inserting competent, after conscious,; and  
(B)in paragraph (5)(A)— (i)in clause (i), by striking or at the end; 
(ii)in clause (ii)— (I)in subclause (I), by striking the semicolon at the end and inserting ; or; 
(II)in subclause (II), by striking ; or and inserting a semicolon; and  (III)by striking subclause (III); and 
(iii)by adding at the end the following:   (iii)an identifiable individual engaging in sexually explicit conduct; and; and  
(2)in subsection (b)(3)(A)(i), by striking $150,000 and inserting $500,000.
