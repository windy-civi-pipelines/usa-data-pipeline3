Title: 
Official Title: To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to provide for eligibility for coverage of home health services under the Medicare program on the basis of a need for occupational therapy.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Medicare Home Health Accessibility Act.

Section 2:
2.Eligibility for home health services on the basis of a need for occupational therapy(a)Part ASection 1814(a)(2)(C) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395f(a)(2)(C)) is amended by striking or speech therapy and all that follows through need occupational therapy and inserting , occupational, or speech therapy.(b)Part BSection 1835(a)(2)(A) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395n(a)(2)(A)) is amended by striking or speech therapy and all that follows through need occupational therapy and inserting , occupational, or speech therapy.(c)Effective dateThe amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall apply to home health services provided on or after January 1, 2026.
