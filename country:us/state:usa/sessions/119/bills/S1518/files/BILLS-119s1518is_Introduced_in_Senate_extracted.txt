Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Strengthening America’s Turning Point Act.

Section 2:
2.Saratoga National Battlefield Park(a)RedesignationThe Saratoga National Historical Park shall be known and designated as the Saratoga National Battlefield Park.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Saratoga National Historical Park shall be deemed to be a reference to the Saratoga National Battlefield Park.
