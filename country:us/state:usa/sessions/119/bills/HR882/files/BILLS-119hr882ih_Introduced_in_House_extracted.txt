Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit the hiring of individuals who are not citizens of the United States to administer an election for Federal office in a State or local jurisdiction, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the No Foreign Persons Administering Our Elections Act.

Section 2:
2.Requirements with respect to hiring of election administrators for an election for Federal office(a)In generalA State or local jurisdiction may not hire an individual who is not a citizen of the United States to administer an election for Federal office in the State or local jurisdiction.(b)Effective dateThis section shall apply with respect to any election for Federal office held on or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
