Title: 
Official Title: To redesignate certain facilities at Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park in honor of Congressman Bill Pascrell, Jr.
Number of Sections: 1
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Redesignation of Paterson Great Falls NHP facilities(a)Great Falls Scenic Overlook Trail BridgeGreat Falls Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge at Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park shall hereafter be known and designated as Bill Pascrell, Jr. Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge.(b)Overlook ParkOverlook Park at Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park shall hereafter be known and designated as Bill Pascrell, Jr. Overlook Park.(c)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to—(1)Great Falls Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge shall be deemed to be a reference to Bill Pascrell, Jr. Scenic Overlook Trail Bridge; and(2)Overlook Park shall be deemed to be a reference to Bill Pascrell, Jr. Overlook Park.
