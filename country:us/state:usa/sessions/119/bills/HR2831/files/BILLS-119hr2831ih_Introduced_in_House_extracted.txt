Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Small Business Investment Act of 1958 to increase the maximum loan amount for certain loans.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Small Business Energy Loan Enhancement Act.

Section 2:
2.Increase in certain maximum loan amounts(a)In generalSection 502(2)(A) of the Small Business Investment Act of 1958 (15 U.S.C. 696(2)(A)) is amended—(1)in clause (iv), by striking $5,500,000 and inserting $10,000,000; and(2)in clause (v), by striking $5,500,000 and inserting $10,000,000.(b)ReportNot later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, and annually thereafter, the Administrator of the Small Business Administration shall submit to Congress a report that details, for the year covered by the report, the industries and geographic areas with respect to which the Administrator has made loans under clauses (iv) and (v) of section 502(2)(A) of the Small Business Investment Act of 1958 (15 U.S.C. 696(2)(A)), as amended by subsection (a).
