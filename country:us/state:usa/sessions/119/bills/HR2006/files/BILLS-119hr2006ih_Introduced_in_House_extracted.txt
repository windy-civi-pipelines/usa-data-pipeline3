Title: 
Official Title: To codify Executive Order 14210 relating to implementing the President’s Department of Government Efficiency workforce optimization initiative.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Department of Government Efficiency Act or the DOGE Act.

Section 2:
2.Codification of Executive Order 14210Executive Order 14210 (90 Fed. Reg. 9669, relating to implementing the President's Department of Government Efficiency workforce optimization initiative) shall have the force and effect of law.
