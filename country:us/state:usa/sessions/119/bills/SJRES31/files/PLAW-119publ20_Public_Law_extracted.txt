PUBLIC LAW 119–20—JUNE 20, 2025
139 STAT. 71
Public Law119–20
119th Congress



Joint Resolution
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to “Review of Final Rule Reclassification of Major Sources as Area Sources Under Section 112 of the Clean Air Act”.June 20, 2025[S.J. Res. 31]

  Resolved   by   the   Senate   and   House   of   Representatives   of   the   United   States   of   America   in   Congress   assembled,

 That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to “Review of Final Rule Reclassification of Major Sources as Area Sources Under Section 112 of the Clean Air Act” (89 Fed. Reg. 73293 (September 10, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.


Approved June 20, 2025.
