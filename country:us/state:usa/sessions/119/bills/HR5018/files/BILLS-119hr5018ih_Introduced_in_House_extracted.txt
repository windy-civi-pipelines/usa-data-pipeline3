Title: 
Official Title: To authorize use of amounts from emergency solutions grants under the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act to provide safe overnight parking facilities for homeless persons, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Naomi Schwartz and Susan Rose Safe Parking Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Safe parking as eligible activity under Emergency Solutions Grant Program (a)In generalSection 415(a) of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act (42 U.S.C. 11374(a)) is amended by adding at the end the following: 
 
(6)Maintenance, operation, insurance, and provision of utilities related to safe parking.. (b)DefinitionSection 411 of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act (42 U.S.C. 11371) is amended— 
(1)by redesignating paragraphs (7) through (9) as paragraphs (8) through (10), respectively; and (2)by inserting after paragraph (6) the following new paragraph: 
 
(7)The term safe parking means an activity that— (A)provides homeless persons living in vehicles, including motor homes, with a safe place to park their vehicles overnight to facilitate a transition to stable housing; and 
(B)provides re-housing and supportive services to such homeless persons..
