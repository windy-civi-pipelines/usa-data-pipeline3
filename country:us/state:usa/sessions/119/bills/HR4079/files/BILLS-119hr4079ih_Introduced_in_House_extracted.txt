Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize grants for first responder training.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Safer Response Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.First responder training programSection 546 of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 290ee–1) is amended—
(1)in subsection (a), by striking tribes and tribal and inserting Tribes and Tribal; (2)in subsections (a), (c), and (d)—
(A)by striking approved or cleared each place it appears and inserting approved, cleared, or otherwise legally marketed; and (B)by striking opioid each place it appears;
(3)in subsection (f)— (A)by striking approved or cleared each place it appears and inserting approved, cleared, or otherwise legally marketed;
(B)in paragraph (1), by striking opioid; (C)in paragraph (2)—
(i)by striking opioid and heroin and inserting opioid, heroin, and other drug; and (ii)by striking opioid overdose and inserting overdose; and
(D)in paragraph (3), by striking opioid and heroin; and (4)in subsection (h), by striking $36,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023 and inserting $57,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030.
