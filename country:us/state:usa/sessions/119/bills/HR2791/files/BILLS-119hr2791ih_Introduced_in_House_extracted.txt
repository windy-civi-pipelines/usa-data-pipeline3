Section 1:
1.Increase to maximum amount of housing loan guaranty entitlement available to certain veteransClause (ii) of section 3703(a)(1)(C) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by striking 25 percent of the Freddie Mac conforming loan limit and inserting 25 percent of the product of the Freddie Mac conforming loan limit multiplied by 1.5.
