1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Protect Small Businesses from Excessive Paperwork Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Modification to filing deadline for certain pre-existing reporting companiesSection 5336(b)(1)(B) of title 31, United States Code, is amended by striking before the effective date of the regulations prescribed under this subsection shall, in a timely manner, and not later than 2 years after the effective date of the regulations prescribed under this subsection, and inserting before January 1, 2024, shall, not later than January 1, 2026,.
