Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Alpha-gal Allergen Inclusion Act.

Section 2:
2.Adding alpha-gal to definition of major food allergen (a)In generalSection 201(qq) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (21 U.S.C. 321(qq)) is amended by adding at the end the following: 
 
(3) 
(A)Galactose-alpha-1,3-galactose (commonly known as alpha-gal). (B)For purposes of this subparagraph, the term galactose-alpha-1,3-galactose includes— 
(i)ingredients derived from non-catarrhine primate mammals; and (ii)red algae in the order Gigartinales. 
(C)For purposes of this subparagraph, the term galactose-alpha-1,3-galactose does not include mammal-derived ingredients for which the Secretary determines there is no galactose-alpha-1,3-galactose above an established, detectable limit (such as ingredients made from alpha-gal-knockout mammals).. (b)Effective dateThe amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply beginning on the date that is 18 months after the date of enactment of this Act.
