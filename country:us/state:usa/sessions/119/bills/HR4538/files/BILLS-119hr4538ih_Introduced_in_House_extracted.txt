Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit covered entities from requiring consumers to solely use digital monthly statements, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Protecting Against Paperless and Electronic Requirements Act or the PAPER Act.

Section 2:
2.Requirement (a)In generalEach covered entity shall offer each consumer of such entity the option of receiving paper copies of their monthly statements.
(b)ProhibitionA covered entity may not condition the use of any services offered by such entity on the sole use of digital monthly statements by a consumer. (c)Definitions (1)Depository institutionThe term depository institution has the meaning given the term in section 3 of the Federal Deposit Act.
(2)Credit unionThe term credit union has the meaning given the term in section 101 of the Federal Credit Union Act. (3)Covered entityThe term covered entity means a depository institution or a credit union.
