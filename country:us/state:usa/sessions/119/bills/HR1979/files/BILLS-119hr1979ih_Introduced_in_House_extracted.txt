Such a motion shall not be in order after the House has disposed of a motion to proceed with respect to that special message. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the motion to its adoption without intervening motion. A motion to reconsider the vote by which the motion is disposed of shall not be in order.(C)ConsiderationThe approval bill shall be considered as read. All points of order against an approval bill and against its consideration are waived.
