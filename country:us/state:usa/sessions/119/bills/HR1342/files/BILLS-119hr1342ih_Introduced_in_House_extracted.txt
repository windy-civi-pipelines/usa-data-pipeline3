Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit discrimination based on political affiliation in granting disaster assistance.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Stopping Political Discrimination in Disaster Assistance Act.

Section 2:
2.Nondiscrimination in disaster assistanceSection 308(a) of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act (42 U.S.C. 5151(a)) is amended by striking or economic status and inserting economic status, or political affiliation.
