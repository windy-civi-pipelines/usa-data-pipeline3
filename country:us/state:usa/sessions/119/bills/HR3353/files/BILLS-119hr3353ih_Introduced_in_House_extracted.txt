Title: 
Official Title: To increase the penalty for prohibited provision of a phone in a correctional facility, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 3
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Lieutenant Osvaldo Albarati Stopping Prison Contraband Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibited provision of a phoneSection 1791(b) of title 18, United States Code, is amended—
(1)by redesignating paragraphs (4) and (5) as paragraphs (5) and (6), respectively; (2)by inserting after paragraph (3) the following:

(4)in the case of a violation of subsection (a)(1), imprisonment for not more than 2 years, or both, if the object is specified in subsection (d)(1)(F) of this section;; and (3)in paragraph (5), as so redesignated, by inserting , in the case of a violation of subsection (a)(2), before (d)(1)(F).

Section 3:
3.Review of policiesNot later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Director of the Bureau of Prisons shall— (1)conduct a review of the policies of the Bureau of Prisons pertaining to inmates who make, possess, obtain, or attempt to make or obtain a prohibited object, as defined in section 1791(d)(1) of title 18, United States Code; and
(2)update those policies as needed to improve protections for incarcerated individuals and staff.
