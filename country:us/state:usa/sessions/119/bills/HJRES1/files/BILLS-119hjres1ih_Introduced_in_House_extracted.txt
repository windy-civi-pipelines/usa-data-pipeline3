Title: 
Official Title: Proposing an amendment to the Constitution of the United States to require that the Supreme Court of the United States be composed of nine justices.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
That the following article is proposed as an amendment to the Constitution of the United States, which shall be valid to all intents and purposes as part of the Constitution when ratified by the legislatures of three-fourths of the several States within seven years after the date of its submission for ratification: —The Supreme Court of the United States shall be composed of nine justices consisting of one chief justice and eight associate justices..

Section 2:
The Supreme Court of the United States shall be composed of nine justices consisting of one chief justice and eight associate justices.
