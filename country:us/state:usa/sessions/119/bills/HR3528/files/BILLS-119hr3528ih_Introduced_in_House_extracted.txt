Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit the Secretary of State from holding or otherwise
 carrying out the duties of any other Federal position.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Reaffirming Unified Boundaries for Integrity and Oversight Act or the RUBIO Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on Secretary of State holding or otherwise carrying out duties of other
 Federal positionsThe Secretary of State may not hold or otherwise carry out the duties of any other Federal position, and no Federal funds may be obligated or expended for the salaries or expenses of the Secretary if the Secretary so holds or otherwise carries out duties of such other positions.
