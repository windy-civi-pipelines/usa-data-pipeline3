Section 1:
1.Establishing new ZIP CodesNot later than 270 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code for each of the following communities: (1)Eastvale, California. 
(2)Scotland, Connecticut. (3)Cooper City, Florida. 
(4)Miami Lakes, Florida. (5)Ocoee, Florida. 
(6)Village of Estero, Florida. (7)Urbandale, Iowa. 
(8)Mills, Wyoming.
