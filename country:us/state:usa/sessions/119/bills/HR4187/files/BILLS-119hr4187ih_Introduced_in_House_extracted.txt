Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 18, United States Code, to clarify the causation element in the Federal hate crime statute, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Stop Hate Crimes Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Clarification as to causation element in hate crimesSection 249(a) of title 18, United States Code, is amended—
(1)in paragraph (1), in the matter preceding subparagraph (A)— (A)by striking because of and inserting if; and
(B)by inserting after origin of any person the following: was a contributory motivating factor for causing or attempting to cause such injury; and (2)in paragraph (2)(A), in the matter preceding clause (i)—
(A)by striking because of and inserting if; and (B)by inserting after disability of any person the following: was a contributory motivating factor for causing or attempting to cause such injury.
