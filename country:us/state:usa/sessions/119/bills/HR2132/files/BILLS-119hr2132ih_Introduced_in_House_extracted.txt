Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 49, United States Code, to exempt Northern Mariana Islands from certain eligibility requirements for essential air service program, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Marianas Air Service Improvement Act.

Section 2:
2.Exception for locations in Northern Mariana IslandsSection 41731(c) of title 49, United States Code, is amended to read as follows: (c)Exception for locations in Alaska, Hawaii, and Northern Mariana IslandsSubparagraphs (B), (C), and (D) of subsection (a)(1) shall not apply with respect to locations in the State of Alaska, the State of Hawaii, or the Northern Mariana Islands..
