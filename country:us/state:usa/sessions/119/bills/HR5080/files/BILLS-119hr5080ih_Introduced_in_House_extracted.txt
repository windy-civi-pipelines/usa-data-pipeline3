Title: 
Official Title: To redesignate the Department of Defense the Department of War.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Department of War Restoration Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Redesignation of department of defense as department of war(a)Department of warThe Department of Defense is hereby redesignated the Department of War.(b)Secretary of warThe head of the Department of War, as redesignated by subsection (a), is the Secretary of War.(c)ReferencesAny reference to the Department of Defense or the Secretary of Defense in any law, rule regulation, certificate, directive, instruction, or other offi-cial paper in force on the date of enactment of this Act shall be considered to refer and apply to the Department of War and the Secretary of War, respectively.
