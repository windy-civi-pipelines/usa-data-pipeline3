Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Small Business Investment Act of 1958 to increase the amount that may be invested in small business investment companies.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Referred in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Investing in Main Street Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Investment in small business investment companiesSection 302(b) of the Small Business Investment Act of 1958 (15 U.S.C. 682(b)) is amended—(1)in paragraph (1), by striking 5 percent and inserting 15 percent; and(2)in paragraph (2), by striking 5 percent and inserting 15 percent.
