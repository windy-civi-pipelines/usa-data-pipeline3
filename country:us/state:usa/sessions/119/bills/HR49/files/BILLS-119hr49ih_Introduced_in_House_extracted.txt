Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit the use of Federal funds for the HHS Reproductive Healthcare Access Task Force.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the No Pro-Abortion Task Force Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition against use of Federal funds for HHS Reproductive Healthcare Access Task ForceNo Federal funds may be used for—(1)the HHS Reproductive Healthcare Access Task Force, announced by the Secretary of Health and Human Services on January 21, 2022; or(2)any successor or substantially similar task force.
