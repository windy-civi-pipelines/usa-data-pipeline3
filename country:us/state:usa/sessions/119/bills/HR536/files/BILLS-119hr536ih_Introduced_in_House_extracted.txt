Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend the energy credit for qualified biogas property.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Agricultural Environmental Stewardship Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Extension of energy credit for qualified biogas property(a)In generalSection 48(c)(7)(C) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking December 31, 2024 and inserting December 31, 2025.(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by this section shall apply to property the construction of which begins after December 31, 2024.
