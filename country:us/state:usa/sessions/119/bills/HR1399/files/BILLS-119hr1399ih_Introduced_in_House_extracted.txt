Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 to expand the ability of trade associations to solicit contributions from the stockholders and executive or administrative personnel of their member corporations, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Prior Approval Reform Act.

Section 2:
2.Removing requirement for prior approval(a)SolicitationsSection 316(b)(4)(D) of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 (52 U.S.C. 30118(b)(4)(D)) is amended by striking to the extent that and all that follows and inserting a period.(b)Effective DateThe amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to solicitations made on or after January 1, 2025.
