Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 to authorize the funds under the matching grant program for school security to be used to hire school resource officers, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Funding Officers and Resources To Increase Facility Youth Protection Act or the FORTIFY Act.

Section 2:
2.Authorization to hire school resource officers under matching grant program for school security(a)In generalSection 2701 of part AA of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (34 U.S.C. 10551) is amended—(1)in subsection (a)(1), by striking through (9) and inserting through (12); and(2)in subsection (b)—(A)by redesignating paragraph (9) as paragraph (12); and(B)by inserting after paragraph (8) the following:(9)Hiring school resource officers (as such term is defined in section 1709).(10)Purchasing firearms and protective equipment for school resource officers.(11)Purchasing vehicles, including all terrain vehicles, golf carts, scooters, and bicycles, for use by school resource officers..(b)Technical assistance(1)In generalSection 2702 of part AA of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (34 U.S.C. 10552) is amended by adding at the end the following:(c)Technical assistanceThe Attorney General shall provide technical assistance to States, units of local government, and Indian tribes to improve access to grants under this part for rural and geographically underserved areas..(2)Report to CongressSection 2703(a) of part AA of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (34 U.S.C. 10553) is amended—(A)by striking and the activities and inserting the activities; and(B)by inserting before the period at the end the following: , and how the provision of technical assistance under section 2702(c) has improved access to grants under this part for rural and geographically underserved areas.
