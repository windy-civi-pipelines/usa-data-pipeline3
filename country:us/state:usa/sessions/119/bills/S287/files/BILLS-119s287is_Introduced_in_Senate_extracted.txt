Title: 
Official Title: To designate the Federal building located at 225 South Pierre Street in Pierre, South Dakota, as the “Marcella LeBeau Federal Building”, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Marcella LeBeau Recognition Act.

Section 2:
2.Marcella LeBeau Federal Building(a)DesignationThe Federal building located at 225 South Pierre Street in Pierre, South Dakota, shall be known and designated as the Marcella LeBeau Federal Building.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Marcella LeBeau Federal Building.
