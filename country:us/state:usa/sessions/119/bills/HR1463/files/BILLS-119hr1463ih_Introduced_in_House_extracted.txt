1.Prohibition on funding for Food and Drug Administration rule relating to medical devices; laboratory developed testsNotwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be used to implement, administer, or enforce the final rule of the Food and Drug Administration relating to Medical Devices; Laboratory Developed Tests that was submitted for publication in the Federal Register on May 6, 2024 (89 Fed. Reg. 37286), or any substantially similar rule.
