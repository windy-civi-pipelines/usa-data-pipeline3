Title: 
Official Title: To provide for expedited procurement for experimental purposes, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Procurement Reform for Immediate Military Equipment Act or the PRIME Act.

Section 2:
2.Modifications to procurement for experimental purposesSection 4023 of title 10, United States Code, is amended—(1)in subsection (a), by striking ordnance, signal, chemical activity, transportation, energy, medical, space flight, telecommunications, and aeronautical supplies, including parts and accessories, and designs thereof, and inserting demonstrations, prototypes, products, supplies, parts, accessories, auxiliary services, and design for defense-related articles;(2)in subsection (b)—(A)by inserting or modified after may be made; and(B)by inserting prototyping, after greater than necessary for; and(3)by adding at the end the following new subsection:(c)Follow-On production contracts or transactionsPurchases under this section may allow for follow-on production contracts or transactions without the use of competitive procedures or further justification, even if explicit notification was not provided, if a combatant command submits a written determination that the purchased item successfully completed the experiment and intends to field the item..
