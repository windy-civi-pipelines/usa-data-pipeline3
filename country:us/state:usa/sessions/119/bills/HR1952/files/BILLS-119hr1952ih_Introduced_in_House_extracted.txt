Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Future Funding for Agricultural Research, Mentorship, and Education Reauthorization Act or the Future FARMER Act.

Section 2:
2.Extension of grants and fellowships for food and agricultural sciences educationSection 1417(m) of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977 (7 U.S.C. 3152(m)) is amended— 
(1)in paragraph (1), by striking and at the end; (2)in paragraph (2), by striking the period at the end and adding ; and; and 
(3)by adding at the end the following:  (3)$40,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2029..
