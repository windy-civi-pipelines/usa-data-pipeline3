Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to make the exclusion for certain employer payments of student loans under educational assistance programs permanent.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Employer Participation in Repayment Act.

Section 2:
2.Exclusion for certain employer payments of student loans under educational assistance programs made permanent(a)In generalSection 127(c)(1)(B) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking in the case of payments made before January 1, 2026,.(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by this section shall apply to payments made after the date of the enactment of this Act.
