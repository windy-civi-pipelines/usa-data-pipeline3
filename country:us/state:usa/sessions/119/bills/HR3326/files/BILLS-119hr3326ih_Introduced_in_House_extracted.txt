Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit the use of Federal funds to rename the Persian Gulf, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Persian Gulf Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on use of Federal funds to rename Persian Gulf (a)In generalNotwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds are authorized to be appropriated or otherwise made available to rename the Persian Gulf. 
(b)ReferencesNo law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States may refer to the Persian Gulf by a different name without express authorization of Congress.
