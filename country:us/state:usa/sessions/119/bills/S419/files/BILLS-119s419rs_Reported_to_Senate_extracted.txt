Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 to reauthorize grants to support law enforcement officers and families, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Reported to Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Reauthorizing Support and Treatment for Officers in Crisis Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.ReauthorizationSection 1001(a)(21) of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (34 U.S.C. 10261(a)(21)) is amended by striking 2020 through 2024 and inserting 2025 through 2029.
