III119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. RES. 165IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESApril 9, 2025Mr. Sanders submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Veterans' AffairsRESOLUTIONExpressing the sense of the Senate that the plan of President Trump and Elon Musk to fire 83,000 employees of the Department of Veterans Affairs is unacceptable and must be rescinded. Whereas, at the direction of President Trump and Elon Musk, the Department of Veterans Affairs is preparing a plan to fire up to 83,000 employees who provide care and benefits to our veterans: Now, therefore, be it That it is the sense of the Senate that the Department of Veterans Affairs must immediately reject and rescind its Agency Reduction in Force and Reorganization Plan.
