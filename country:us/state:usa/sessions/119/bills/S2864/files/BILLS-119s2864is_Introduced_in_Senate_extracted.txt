Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Wagner-Peyser Act to allow States the flexibility to use
            staffing arrangements that best suit their needs, for employment service
            offices.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Pathways to Paychecks Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Employment service offices
 Section 9 of the Wagner-Peyser Act (29 U.S.C. 49h) is amended by adding at the end the following:
            
                
                    (d)
 States may use State merit staff, or other staff that meet the requirements applicable to Federal contractors, to carry out the duties of employment service offices under this Act.
                
                .

            Short title

            2.
            Employment service offices
 Section 9 of the Wagner-Peyser Act (29 U.S.C. 49h) is amended by adding at the end the following:
            
                
                    (d)
 States may use State merit staff, or other staff that meet the requirements applicable to Federal contractors, to carry out the duties of employment service offices under this Act.
