Title: 
Official Title: To direct the Secretary of Defense to provide fluid or powdered milk to members of the Armed Forces at dining facilities on military installations.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Strengthening our Servicemembers with Milk Act.

Section 2:
2.Provision of milk to members of the Armed Forces at dining facilities on military installations (a)In generalThe Secretary of Defense shall provide fluid or powdered milk to members of the Armed Forces at dining facilities on military installations, in varieties that may include—
(1)unflavored; (2)flavored;
(3)organic; (4)non-organic;
(5)whole; (6)reduced-fat;
(7)low-fat; (8)fat-free;
(9)lactose-free; or (10)any combination thereof.
(b)ProhibitionThe Secretary may not, to carry out this section, purchase fluid or powdered milk from an entity owned or controlled by a foreign adversary, as determined by the Secretary of Commerce under section 7.4 of title 15, Code of Federal Regulations (or successor regulation).
