Section 1:
1.Inclusion of Parkinson’s disease in list of illnesses and diseases deemed to be proximately caused by employment in fire protection activitiesParagraph (2) of section 8143b(b) of title 5, United States Code, is amended— (1)by redesignating subparagraphs (L) through (P) as subparagraphs (M) through (Q), respectively; and 
(2)by inserting after subparagraph (K) the following new subparagraph:  (L)Parkinson’s disease..
