Official Title: To reauthorize the National Flood Insurance Program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the NFIP Extension Act.

Section 2:
2.Reauthorization(a)FinancingSection 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4016(a)) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting November 21, 2025.(b)Program expirationSection 1319 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4026) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting November 21, 2025.(c)Retroactive effective dateIf this Act is enacted after September 30, 2025, the amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall take effect as if enacted on September 30, 2025.
