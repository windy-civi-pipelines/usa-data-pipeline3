Title: 
Official Title: To amend the mission statement of the United States Military Academy to include the phrase
Number of Sections: 3
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Maintaining Academy Culture and Assuring Retention of Tradition, Honor, and Unity of the Republic Act or the MACARTHUR Act.

Section 2:
2.Sense of CongressIt is the sense of Congress that the principles of Duty, Honor, Country should be—(1)deeply embedded in the ethos of the United States Military Academy; and(2)instilled in each cadet.

Section 3:
3.Modification of united states military academy mission statementNot later than 30 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of the Army shall amend the mission statement of the United States Military Academy to include the phrase Duty, Honor, Country.
