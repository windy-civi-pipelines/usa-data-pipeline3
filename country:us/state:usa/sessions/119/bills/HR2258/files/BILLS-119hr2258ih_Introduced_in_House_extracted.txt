Title: 
Official Title: To designate the Maine Forest and Logging Museum, located in Bradley, Maine, as the National Museum of Forestry and Logging History.
Number of Sections: 1
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.National Museum of Forestry and Logging HistoryThe Maine Forest and Logging Museum, located in Bradley, Maine, shall be known and designated as the National Museum of Forestry and Logging History. Any reference in any law, map, regulation, document, record, or other paper of the United States to that museum shall be held to be a reference to the National Museum of Forestry and Logging History.
