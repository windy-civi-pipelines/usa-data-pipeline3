Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit Federal funds from being made available to any law enforcement agency that employs an alien as a law enforcement officer, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Citizen-Only Police Act of 2025 or the COP Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on availability of Federal fundsNo Federal funds may be made available to any law enforcement agency that employs an alien as a law enforcement officer.
