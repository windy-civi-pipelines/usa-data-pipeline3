Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Paula Bohovesky and Joan D’Alessandro Act.

Section 2:
2.Death or Imprisonment for Crimes Against ChildrenSection 3559(d)(1)(A) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by inserting after 14 years the following: or if the victim has not attained the age of 18 years if the conviction involved a sexual offense.
