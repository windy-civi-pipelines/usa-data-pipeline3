================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Eligibility of veterans of Operation End Sweep for Vietnam Service MedalThe Secretary of the military department concerned may, upon the application of an individual who is a veteran who participated in Operation End Sweep, award that individual the Vietnam Service Medal.
