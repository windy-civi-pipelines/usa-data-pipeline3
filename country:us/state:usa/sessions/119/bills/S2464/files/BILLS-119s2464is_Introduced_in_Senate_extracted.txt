Title: 
Official Title: To allow the Comptroller of the Currency and the Board of Governors of the
            Federal Reserve System to increase the aggregate amount of investments that a national
            banking association and a State member bank may make to promote the public welfare, and
            for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Community Investment and Prosperity Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Investments to promote the public welfare
            
                (a)
                Revised Statutes
 The paragraph designated as the Eleventh of section 5136 of the Revised Statutes of the United States (12 U.S.C. 24) is amended, in the fifth sentence, by striking 15 each place the term appears and inserting 20.
            
            
                (b)
                Federal Reserve Act
 The 23rd paragraph of section 9 of the Federal Reserve Act (12 U.S.C. 338a) is amended, in the fifth sentence, by striking 15 each place the term appears and inserting 20.
