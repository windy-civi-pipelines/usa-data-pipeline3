Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the No Bail Post-Jail Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Denial for pretrial release for prior incarceration
 Section 3124(e) of title 18, United States Code is amended by adding at the end the following:
            
                
                    (4)
 A person shall be considered to pose a danger to the safety of the community and be ineligible for release if the judicial officer finds that the person—
                    
                        (A)
 is charged with a felony offense;
                    
                    
                        (B)
 is an adult or a juvenile charged as an adult with regard to the offense; and
                    
                    
                        (C)
 has a prior felony conviction for a crime of violence that resulted in the person serving not less than 30 days in a State or Federal correctional facility, not including any period of pretrial detention without conviction.
                    
                
                .
