Section 1:
1.Implementation of Land Protection Plan for Muleshoe National Wildlife Refuge prohibitedThe Secretary of the Interior may not finalize, implement, administer, or enforce the Land Protection Plan described in the document published by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service titled Final Land Protection Plan & Environmental Assessment Muleshoe National Wildlife Refuge (February 2023).
