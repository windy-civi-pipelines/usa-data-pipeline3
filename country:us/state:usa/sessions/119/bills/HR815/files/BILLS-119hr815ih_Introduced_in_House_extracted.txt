Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to extend expensing of environmental remediation costs.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Brownfields Redevelopment Tax Incentive Reauthorization Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Extension of expensing of environmental remediation costs(a)In generalSection 198(h) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking incurred after December 31, 2011 and inserting the following:incurred—(1)after December 31, 2011, and before January 1, 2025, or(2)after December 31, 2028..(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to expenditures paid or incurred after December 31, 2024.
