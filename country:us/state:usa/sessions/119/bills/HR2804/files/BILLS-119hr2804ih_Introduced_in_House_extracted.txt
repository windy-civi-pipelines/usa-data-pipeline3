Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Small Business Act to codify the Rule of Two, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Protecting Small Business Competitions Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Codification of the Rule of TwoSection 15(j) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 644(j)) is amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph: 
 
(4)Rule of TwoEach contract, task order, or delivery order for the purchase of goods or services that has an anticipated value greater than the simplified acquisition threshold shall be reserved for small business concerns when the contracting officer reasonably expects— (A)to obtain offers from two or more responsible small business concerns; and 
(B)to award such contract, task order, or delivery order at a fair market price..
