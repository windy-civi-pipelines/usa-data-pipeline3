Title: 
Official Title: To amend title V of the Social Security Act to extend funding for the family-to-family health information centers.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Family-to-Family Reauthorization Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Five-year extension of funding for family-to-family health information centersSection 501(c)(1)(A) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 701(c)(1)(A)) is amended— 
(1)in clause (vii), by striking and at the end; (2)in clause (viii), by striking the period at the end and inserting a semicolon; and 
(3)by adding at the end the following:   (ix)$6,000,000 for the period beginning on April 1, 2025, and ending on September 30, 2025; and 
(x)$9,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2029. .
