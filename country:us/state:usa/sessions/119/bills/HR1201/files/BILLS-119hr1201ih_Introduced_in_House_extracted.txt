1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Doctors in our Borders Act.

2.Increased number of Conrad 30 waiversSection 214(l)(1)(B) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1184(l)(1)(B)) is amended by striking to exceed 30 and inserting to exceed 100.
