Title: 
Official Title: To direct the Secretary of Health and Human Services to issue guidance on payment under the Medicare program for certain items involving artificial intelligence.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Maintaining Innovation and Safe Technologies Act.

Section 2:
2.Guidance on Medicare payment for certain items involving artificial intelligenceNot later than January 1, 2027, the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall use existing communications mechanisms to issue guidance on requirements for payment under part B of title XVIII of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395j et seq.) for remote monitoring devices, such as continuous glucose monitors, that—(1)use an artificial intelligence component (such as a continuous adjustment component); and(2)transmit information to a health care provider for purposes of management and treatment of an individual.
