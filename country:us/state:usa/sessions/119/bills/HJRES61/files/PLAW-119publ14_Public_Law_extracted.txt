PUBLIC LAW 119–14—MAY 23, 2025

119th Congress



Joint Resolution
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to “National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Rubber Tire Manufacturing”.May 23, 2025[H.J. Res. 61]

  Resolved   by   the   Senate   and   House   of   Representatives   of   the   United   States   of   America   in   Congress   assembled,

 That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency relating to “National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Rubber Tire Manufacturing” (89 Fed. Reg. 94886 (November 29, 2024)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.


Approved May 23, 2025.
