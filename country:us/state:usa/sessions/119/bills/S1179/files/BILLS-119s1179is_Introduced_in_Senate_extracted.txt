Title: 
Official Title: To name the community-based outpatient clinic of the Department of Veterans Affairs in Las Cruces, New Mexico, the
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Las Cruces Bataan Memorial Clinic Act.

Section 2:
2.Name of Department of Veterans Affairs community-based outpatient clinic in Las Cruces, New Mexico(a)NameThe community-based outpatient clinic of the Department of Veterans Affairs in Las Cruces, New Mexico, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known and designated as the Las Cruces Bataan Memorial Clinic.(b)ReferenceAny reference in any law, regulation, map, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the clinic referred to in subsection (a) shall be considered to be a reference to the Las Cruces Bataan Memorial Clinic.
