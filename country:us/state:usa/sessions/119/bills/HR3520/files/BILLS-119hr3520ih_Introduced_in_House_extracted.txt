Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Controlled Substances Act to provide for the scheduling of tianeptine as a schedule III substance, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Scheduling Tianeptine and Analogues Now to Defend Against Emerging Opioids Act or the STAND Against Emerging Opioids Act.

Section 2:
2.Scheduling of tianeptine (a)In generalSection 202(c) of the Controlled Substances Act (21 U.S.C. 812(c)) is amended by adding at the end of schedule III the following:

(f)Tianeptine, including its analogues, salts, isomers, and salts of isomers.. (b)Effective dateThe amendment made by subsection (a) shall become effective on the date that is 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act.
