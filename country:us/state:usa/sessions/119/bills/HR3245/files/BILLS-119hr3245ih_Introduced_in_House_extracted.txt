Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the American Privacy Restoration Act.

Section 2:
2.Repeal of USA PATRIOT ActThe USA PATRIOT Act (Public Law 107–56; 115 Stat. 272) is repealed, and each provision of law amended by such Act is amended to read as such provision read on October 25, 2001.
