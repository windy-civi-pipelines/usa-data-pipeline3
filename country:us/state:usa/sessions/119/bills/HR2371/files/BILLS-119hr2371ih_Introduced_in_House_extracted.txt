Title: 
Official Title: To adjust the boundaries of the Golden Gate National Recreation Area to include the Scarper Ridge property.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Scarper Ridge Golden Gate National Recreation Area Boundary Adjustment Act.

Section 2:
2.Scarper Ridge boundary adjustmentSection 2(a)(2) of Public Law 92–589 (16 U.S.C. 460bb–1(a)(2)) is amended by adding at the end the following:

(F)Land generally depicted as Proposed Boundary Addition on the map entitled Golden Gate National Recreation Area Proposed Boundary Addition, numbered 641/193973, and dated July 2024.
