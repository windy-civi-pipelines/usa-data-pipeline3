Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 49, United States Code, to include aircraft deicing storage facilities in the definition of airport development, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Reported in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Facility for Runway Operations and Safe Transportation Act or the FROST Act.

Section 2:
2.Deicing equipment protectionSection 47102(3)(B)(v) of title 49, United States Code, is amended by striking and storage facilities for the equipment and fluids.
