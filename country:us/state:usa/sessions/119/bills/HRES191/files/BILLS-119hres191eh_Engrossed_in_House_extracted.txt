IV 
119th CONGRESS 
1st Session 
H. RES. 191 
In the House of Representatives, U. S., 
 
March 5, 2025 
 
RESOLUTION 
 
 
 
That the House has heard with profound sorrow of the death of the Honorable Sylvester Turner, a Representative from the State of Texas. That the Clerk communicate these resolutions to the Senate and transmit a copy thereof to the family of the deceased. 
That when the House adjourns today, it adjourn as a further mark of respect to the memory of the deceased.   Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
