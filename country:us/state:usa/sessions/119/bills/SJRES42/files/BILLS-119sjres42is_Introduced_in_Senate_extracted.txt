Section 1:
That the following proposed export to Israel is prohibited: (1)The export of the following firearms, parts, and components controlled under Category I of the United States Munitions List in the amount of $1,000,000 or more and described in Transmittal No. DDTC 23–086, which was submitted to Congress pursuant to section 36(c) of the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2776(c)) and communication of which was published in the Congressional Record on March 24, 2025 (Executive Communication 509): 3,200 DDM4, 11.5” barrel length, 5.56mm and 2,000 MK18, 10.3” barrel length, 5.56mm fully automatic rifles to Lavi BBG Ltd. in Israel for ultimate end use by the Israel National Police.

RES. 42IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 27, 2025Mr. Sanders introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Foreign RelationsJOINT RESOLUTIONProviding for congressional disapproval of the proposed export of certain defense articles to Israel. That the following proposed export to Israel is prohibited: (1)The export of the following firearms, parts, and components controlled under Category I of the United States Munitions List in the amount of $1,000,000 or more and described in Transmittal No. DDTC 23–086, which was submitted to Congress pursuant to section 36(c) of the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2776(c)) and communication of which was published in the Congressional Record on March 24, 2025 (Executive Communication 509): 3,200 DDM4, 11.5” barrel length, 5.56mm and 2,000 MK18, 10.3” barrel length, 5.56mm fully automatic rifles to Lavi BBG Ltd. in Israel for ultimate end use by the Israel National Police.
