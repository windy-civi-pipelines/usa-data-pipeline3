Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit importation of commercially produced fresh citrus fruit originating from the People’s Republic of China.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the U.S. Citrus Protection Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on importation of commercially produced fresh citrus fruit originating from the People’s Republic of China (a)In generalNotwithstanding any other provision of law, articles of commercially produced fresh citrus fruit originating from the People’s Republic of China may not be imported into the United States.
(b)Effective dateThis Act shall take effect on the date that is 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act.
