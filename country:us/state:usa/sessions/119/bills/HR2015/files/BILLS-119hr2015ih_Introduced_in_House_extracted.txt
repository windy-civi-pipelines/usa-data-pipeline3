Title: 
Official Title: To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to prohibit hospitals from taking into account vaccination status in selecting organ recipients.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Guaranteeing Individual Fairness in Transplants Act of 2025 or the GIFT Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Prohibiting hospitals from taking into account vaccination status in selecting organ recipientsSection 1866(a)(1) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395cc(a)(1)) is amended—(1)in subparagraph (X), by striking and at the end;(2)in subparagraph (Y), by striking the period at the end and inserting , and; and(3)by inserting after subparagraph (Y) the following new subparagraph:(Z)in the case of a hospital, critical access hospital, or rural emergency hospital, to not take into account the vaccination status of an individual when determining which individual should receive an organ transplant..
