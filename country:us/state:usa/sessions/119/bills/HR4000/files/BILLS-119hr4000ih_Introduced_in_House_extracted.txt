Section 1:
1.Prohibition on access or entrance to Department of Energy sites and facilities by non-U.S. citizens 
(a)ProhibitionNotwithstanding any other provision of law, except as provided in subsection (b), a person who is not a citizen of the United States may not access or enter any site or facility owned, operated, or leased by the Department of Energy. (b)ExceptionThe prohibition under subsection (a) shall not apply with respect to any person who is a citizen of Australia, Canada, New Zealand, or the United Kingdom. 
(c)Effective dateThe prohibition under subsection (a) shall take effect on the date that is 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act. (d)ImplementationNot later than 60 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Energy shall revise any regulations, guidelines, policies, and procedures as necessary to carry out this Act.
