Title: 
Official Title: To nullify Presidential Proclamation 10606, establishing Baaj Nwaavjo I’tah Kukveni-Ancestral Footprints of the Grand Canyon National Monument and withdrawing certain land in Arizona from mineral entry, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Northern Arizona Protection Act.

Section 2:
2.Restriction on use of Antiquities Act in certain areas in Arizona  (a) Nullification of Presidential Proclamation Presidential Proclamation 10606 of August 8, 2023 (88 Fed. Reg. 55331, relating to the establishment of the Baaj Nwaavjo I’tah Kukveni-Ancestral Footprints of the Grand Canyon National Monument), shall have no force or effect.
      

        (b)
        Restriction on future monument designations
 No extension or establishment of national monuments within the area generally depicted as Baaj Nwaavjo I’tah Kukveni-Ancestral Footprints of the Grand Canyon National Monument on the map titled Baaj Nwaavjo I’tah Kukveni-Ancestral Footprints of the Grand Canyon National Monument and dated August 5, 2023, may be undertaken except by express authorization of Congress.
