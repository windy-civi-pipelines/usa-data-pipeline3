Title: 
Official Title: To clarify the treatment of certain tips for purposes of the tax deduction
            for qualified tips.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the No Tax on Large Party Tips Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Treatment of certain tips
            
                (a)
                In general
 For purposes of section 224(d)(2)(A) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986—
                
                    (1)
 any tip automatically added to a customer’s bill at the time of payment, and
                
                
                    (2)
 any tip that is a suggested tip prompted by a business,
                
                shall be treated as paid
                    voluntarily.
