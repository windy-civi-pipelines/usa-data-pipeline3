Section 1:
1.Short titleThis resolution may be cited as the Life Month Resolution.

Section 2:
2.Life MonthThat the Senate and the House of Representatives—(1)designate a Life Month annually;(2)recognize that every human life is a sacred gift from God, possessing inherent dignity and worth;(3)reaffirm the fundamental truth recognized in the Declaration of Independence that life is an unalienable right;(4)commend individuals, parents, families, faith-based organizations, and communities who work to promote a culture of life; and(5)urge policymakers to defend the sanctity of human life by enacting laws that protect the unborn and provide resources to empower women and families to choose life.
