Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Food Security Act of 1985 to address emissions of certain greenhouse gases and carbon storage through conservation incentive contracts.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Conservation and Regenerative Optimization Practices for Farming Act or the CROP for Farming Act.

Section 2:
2.Conservation incentive contractsSection 1240B(j) of the Food Security Act of 1985 (16 U.S.C. 3839aa–2(j)) is amended—(1)in paragraph (1)(A), by striking concerns and inserting concerns, which may include emissions of nitrous oxide or methane (referred to in this subsection as greenhouse gas emissions) or storage of carbon in plants or soil (referred to in this subsection as carbon storage),; and(2)in paragraph (2)(A)(ii)(I), by striking conservation and inserting conservation, reductions of greenhouse gas emissions, or increased levels of carbon storage.
