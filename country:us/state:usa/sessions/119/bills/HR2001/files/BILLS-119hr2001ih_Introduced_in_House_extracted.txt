Section 1:
1.Action for Dental HealthSection 340G(f) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 256g(f)) is amended by striking $13,903,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023 and inserting $15,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030, to remain available until expended.
