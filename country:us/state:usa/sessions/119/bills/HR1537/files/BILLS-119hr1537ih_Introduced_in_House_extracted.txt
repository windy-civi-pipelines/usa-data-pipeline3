Title: 
Official Title: To permit individuals 70 years of age or older to opt out of jury service in the Superior Court of the District of Columbia.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the District of Columbia Superior Court Jury Duty for Seniors Opt Out Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Permitting individuals 70 years of age or older to opt out of jury service in Superior Court of the District of ColumbiaSection 11–1908(b), District of Columbia Official Code, is amended— 
(1)by striking or (4) and inserting (4); and (2)by striking the period at the end of the first sentence and inserting ; or (5) excluded by the Court upon the request of the individual if the individual is 70 years of age or older..
