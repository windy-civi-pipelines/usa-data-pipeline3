IV119th CONGRESS1st SessionH. RES. 643IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESAugust 8, 2025Mr. Gimenez (for himself and Mrs. Biggs of South Carolina) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on EthicsRESOLUTIONRemoving a certain Member from a certain standing committee of the House of Representatives. 
Whereas clause 1 of rule XXIII of the Rules of the House of Representatives provides, A Delegate, Resident Commissioner, officer, or employee of the House shall behave at all times in a manner that shall reflect creditably on the House.;  Whereas, at an event in Mexico City, Mexico, Representative Delia Ramirez stated I am a proud Guatemalan before I am an American; and
         
Whereas Representative Ramirez should be removed from her assignment to the Committee on Homeland Security in light of conduct she has exhibited: Now, therefore, be it That the following named Member be, and is hereby, removed from the following standing committee of the House of Representatives: 

Committee on Homeland Security:Mrs. Ramirez.
