2.Reauthorization of Healthy Start InitiativeSection 330H(e)(1) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 254c–8(e)(1)) is amended—(1)by striking appropriated and inserting the following:appropriated—(A);(2)in subparagraph (A), as so inserted, by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and; and(3)by adding at the end the following:(B)$145,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030..
