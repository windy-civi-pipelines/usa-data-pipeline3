Section 1:
1.Nullification of interagency guidanceThe final interagency guidance issued by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, the Comptroller of the Currency, and the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation titled Principles for Climate-Related Financial Risk Management for Large Financial Institutions (published October 24, 2023) shall have no force or effect and such agencies may not issue any substantially similar guidance.
