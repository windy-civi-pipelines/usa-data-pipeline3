Title: 
Official Title: To reauthorize the Congressional Award Act.
Number of Sections: 3
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Congressional Award Program Reauthorization Act.

Section 2:
2.Termination(a)In generalSection 108 of the Congressional Award Act (2 U.S.C. 808) is amended by striking October 1, 2023 and inserting October 1, 2028.(b)Retroactive effective dateThe amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as if enacted on October 1, 2023.

Section 3:
3.Other amendmentsSection 102 of the Congressional Award Act (2 U.S.C. 802) is amended—(1)in subsection (a), by striking Each medal shall consist of gold-plate over bronze, rhodium over bronze, or bronze and shall be struck in accordance with subsection (f).; and(2)in subsection (f)(1), in the second sentence, by striking Subject to subsection (a), the and inserting The.
