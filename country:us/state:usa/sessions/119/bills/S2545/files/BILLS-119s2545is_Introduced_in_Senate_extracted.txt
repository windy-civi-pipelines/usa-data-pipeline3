II119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. 2545IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESJuly 30, 2025Mr. Padilla (for himself and Mr. Cramer) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban AffairsA BILLTo amend the Federal Credit Union Act to provide for certain ways in which credit unions may be Agent members of the National Credit Union Administration Central Liquidity Facility.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the NCUA Central Liquidity Facility Enhancements Act.2.Agent membership Section 304(b)(2) of the Federal Credit Union Act (12 U.S.C. 1795c(b)(2)) is amended by striking all those credit unions and inserting such credit unions as the Board may in its discretion determine .
