Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to prohibit the admission of Chinese nationals as nonimmigrant students, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Stop Chinese Communist Prying by Vindicating Intellectual Safeguards in Academia Act of 2025 or the Stop CCP VISAs Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on admission of Chinese nationals as nonimmigrant studentsSection 214 of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1184) is amended by adding at the end the following: (s)Prohibition on admission of Chinese nationals as nonimmigrant studentsAn alien who is a national of the People's Republic of China may not be issued a visa or otherwise provided status as a nonimmigrant under section 101(a)(15)(F), (J), or (M), for the purpose of conducting research or pursuing a course of study. .
