Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Government Accountability Office District of Columbia Home Rule Act.

Section 2:
2.Repeal of authorities of Government Accountability Office over District of Columbia Government (a)Treatment of District government as Federal agencySection 701(1) of title 31, United States Code, is amended by striking includes the District of Columbia government but. 
(b)Annual audit 
(1)In generalTitle 31, United States Code, is amended by striking section 715. (2)Clerical amendmentThe table of sections of chapter 7 of such title is amended by striking the item relating to section 715.

(e)Response to reportsSection 720(a) of such title is amended by striking or the District of Columbia government.
