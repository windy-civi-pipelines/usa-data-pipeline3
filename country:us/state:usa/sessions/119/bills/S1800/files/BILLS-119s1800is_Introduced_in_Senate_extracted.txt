Title: 
Official Title: To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to modify the criteria for
            designation of rural emergency hospitals.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Rural Health Sustainability Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.
            Modification of criteria for designation of rural emergency hospitals
 Section 1861(kkk)(3) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395x(kkk)(3)) is amended—
            
                (1)
 in the matter preceding subparagraph (A), by striking as of the date of the enactment of this subsection and inserting as of January 1, 2014;
            
            
                (2)
 in subparagraph (A), by striking or at the end;   (3) in subparagraph (B), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; or; and
            
            
                (4)
 by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:   (C) was designated as rural by the Office of Rural Health Policy of the Health Resources and Services Administration.
                    
                    .
