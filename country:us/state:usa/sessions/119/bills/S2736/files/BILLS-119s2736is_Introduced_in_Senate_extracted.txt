Title: 
Official Title: To require the Administrator of General Services to display or inscribe the national motto of the United States on all Federal buildings, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the In God We Trust Act.

Section 2:
2.Display of national motto on Federal buildings(a)Definition of public buildingIn this section, the term public building has the meaning given the term in section 3301(a) of title 40, United States Code.(b)RequirementNot later than 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the Administrator of General Services shall inscribe or display in a place of prominence on every public building the national motto of the United States, as described in section 302 of title 36, United States Code.
