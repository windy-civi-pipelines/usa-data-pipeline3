Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 23, United States Code, to include education on trailer safety in State highway safety programs.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Trailer Safety Improvement Act.

Section 2:
2.Trailer safety programsSection 402(a)(2)(A)(xiv) of title 23, United States Code, is amended by inserting , prevent improper and unsafe use of light-duty and medium-duty trailers, and educate the public about required trailer safety equipment and preventive maintenance after unsecured vehicle loads.
