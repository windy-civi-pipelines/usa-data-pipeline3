Section 1:
1.Maximum monthly allowances of milk under WICSection 17 of the Child Nutrition Act of 1966 (42 U.S.C. 1786) is amended by adding at the end the following:  (t)Maximum monthly allowances of milkThe maximum monthly allowances of milk for the following food packages described in section 246.10(e) of title 7, Code of Federal Regulations are:
(1)For Food Package IV, 16 quarts. (2)For Food Package V, 22 quarts.
(3)For Food Package VI, 22 quarts. (4)For Food Package VII, 24 quarts..
