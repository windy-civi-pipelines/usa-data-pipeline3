Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Taking Account of Bureaucrats’ Spending Act of 2025 or the TABS Act of 2025.

Section 2:

(iv)by striking Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection and inserting Consumer Financial Empowerment Agency (hereinafter in this section referred to as the Agency); (C)in subsection (b)(5), by amending subparagraph (A) to read as follows:
