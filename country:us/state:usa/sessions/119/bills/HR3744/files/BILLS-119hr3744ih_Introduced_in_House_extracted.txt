1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Research Integrity and Foreign Influence Prevention Act.

Section 2:
2.Clarification of definition of foreign country for purposes of malign foreign talent recruitment restrictionParagraph (2) of section 10638 of title VI of division B of the Research and Development, Competition, and Innovation Act (Public Law 117–167; 42 U.S.C. 19237) is amended by inserting , including, beginning on January 1, 2026, any special administrative region within such a covered foreign country of concern or any other territory that the United States recognizes as being under the control of such a covered foreign country of concern after Iran.
