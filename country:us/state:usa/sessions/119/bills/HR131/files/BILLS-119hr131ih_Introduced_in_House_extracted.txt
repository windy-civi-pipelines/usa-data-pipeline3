Official Title: To make certain modifications to the repayment for the Arkansas Valley Conduit in the State of Colorado.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Finish the Arkansas Valley Conduit Act.

Section 2:
2.Arkansas Valley Conduit, ColoradoPublic Law 87–590 (76 Stat. 389) is amended—(1)in subsection (c) of the first section (76 Stat. 390; 123 Stat. 1320), in the second sentence—(A)by inserting , without interest, before in an amount;(B)by striking that is comprised of revenue generated by payments and inserting , which payment shall only consist of funding provided during construction from any person other than the Secretary; (C)by inserting of before revenue that; and(D)by inserting over a period of 100 years after project facilities; and(2)in section 2(b)(3)(A) (76 Stat. 390; 123 Stat. 1321)—(A)by striking plus interest; and(B)by striking this section and inserting subsection (c) of the first section.
