Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Small Business Act to require a limit on the number of small business lending companies, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Capping Excessive Awarding of SBLC Entrants Act or the CEASE Act.

Section 2:
2.Limitation on number of small business lending companiesSection 23 of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 650) is amended by adding at the end the following:(k)Limitation on number of small business lending companiesThe Administrator shall ensure that not more than 16 small business lending companies that are not nonprofit entities are authorized to make loans under section 7 at any time..
