Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act to include additional ZIP Codes with respect to claims relating to Manhattan Project waste.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the St. Louis RECA Readjustment Act.

Section 2:
2.Radiation exposure compensation claims relating to Manhattan Project waste (a)Inclusion of additional ZIP CodesSection 5A(d)(1) of the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act (Public Law 101–426; 42 U.S.C. 2210 note), as added by section 100204 of Public Law 119–21, is amended by inserting 63106, 63107 after 63074,. 
(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect as if included in the enactment of Public Law 119–21.
