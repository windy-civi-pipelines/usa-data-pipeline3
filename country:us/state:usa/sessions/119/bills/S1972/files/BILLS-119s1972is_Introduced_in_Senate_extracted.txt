Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit Federal funding for research centers and laboratories in which nationals of countries of concern conduct agricultural research.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Bioweapon Prevention Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on funding for research centers and laboratories in which nationals of countries of concern conduct agricultural research(a)In generalNo Federal funding shall be available to any research center or laboratory in which a national of a country of concern conducts agricultural research.(b)Definition of country of concernIn this section, the term country of concern means—(1)the Republic of Cuba;(2)the Islamic Republic of Iran;(3)the Russian Federation;(4)the People's Republic of China, including the Special Administrative Region of Hong Kong and the Special Administrative Region of Macau;(5)the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela; and(6)the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
