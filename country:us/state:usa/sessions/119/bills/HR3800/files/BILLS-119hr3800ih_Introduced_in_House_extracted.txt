Title: 
Official Title: To amend the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 to prohibit the deferral of the issuance of letters of map revision based on the placement of fill or conditional letters of map revision based on the placement of fill, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Keeping Homeownership Costs Down Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on the deferral of the issuance of letters of map revision based on the placement of fill (a)In generalSection 1360 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4101) is amended by adding at the end the following: 
 
(k)Prohibition on the deferral of the issuance of letters of map revision based on the placement of fill 
(1)In generalThe Administrator may not defer the issuance of any— (A)letters of map revision based on the placement of fill; or 
(B)conditional letters of map revision based on the placement of fill. (2)Rule of constructionNothing in this subsection shall be construed to affect the review, determination, or issuance of the letters described in paragraph (1).. 
(b)RepealThe amendment made by this Act shall be repealed on the date on which, pursuant to section 7 of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (16 U.S.C. 1536) the Administrator of the Federal Emergency Management Agency fully implements the requirements contained within the biological opinion from the Director of the Fish and Wildlife Service and the biological opinion from the Assistant Administrator for Fisheries of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration issued separately with respect to letters of map revision based on the placement of fill and conditional letters of map revision based on the placement of fill issued pursuant to the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968.
