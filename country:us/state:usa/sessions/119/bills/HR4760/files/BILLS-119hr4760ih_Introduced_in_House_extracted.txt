Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Natural Gas Act to clarify the treatment of bunkering as an export, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Cutting LNG Bunkering Red Tape Act.

Section 2:
2.Treatment of bunkeringSection 3 of the Natural Gas Act (15 U.S.C. 717b) is amended by adding at the end the following: 
 
(g)BunkeringFor purposes of this section, the bunkering of natural gas for use by the receiving vessel as marine fuel shall not be considered an export unless the transfer of such natural gas to the receiving vessel occurs in the territorial sea or inland waters of a foreign country, regardless of the flag or country of registry of either vessel..
