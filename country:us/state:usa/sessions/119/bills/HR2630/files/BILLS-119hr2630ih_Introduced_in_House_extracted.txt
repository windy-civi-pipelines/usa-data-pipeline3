Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Advancing Research to Prevent Suicide Act to expand the areas of focus regarding childhood suicide, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Youth Suicide Prevention Research Act.

Section 2:
2.NSF research regarding childhood suicideSubsection (a) of section 3 of the Advancing Research to Prevent Suicide Act (42 U.S.C. 1862v) is amended—(1)in paragraph (4), by striking and after the semicolon;(2)by redesignating paragraph (5) as paragraph (7); and(3)by inserting after paragraph (4) the following new paragraphs:(5)basic understanding of the role of adverse childhood experiences;(6)basic understanding of the role of toxic stress in childhood; and.
