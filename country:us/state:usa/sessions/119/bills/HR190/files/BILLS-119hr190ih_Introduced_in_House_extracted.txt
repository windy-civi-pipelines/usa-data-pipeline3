Title: 
Official Title: To provide for expedited removal of certain illegal aliens.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Sending Evading Non-Documented Threats Home Especially Migrants Biden Accepted Carelessly and Knowingly Act of 2025 or the SEND THEM BACK Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Expedited removal of certain illegal aliens(a)In generalNotwithstanding any other provision of law, an alien who entered the United States illegally on or since January 20, 2021, shall be subject to expedited removal, even if such alien indicated an intention to apply for asylum or a fear of persecution. (b)ExceptionSubsection (a) shall not apply to any alien who is currently a member of the Armed Forces of the United States as of January 1, 2025.
