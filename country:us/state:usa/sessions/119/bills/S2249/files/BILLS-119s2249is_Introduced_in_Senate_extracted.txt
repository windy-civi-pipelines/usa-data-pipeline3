Title: 
Official Title: To expand eligibility for special overtime pay to U.S. Border Patrol agents
            classified above grade GS–12.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Border Patrol Supervisors Retention Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Establishment of higher rates of regularly
                scheduled overtime pay for certain U.S. Border Patrol agents
 Section 5550(h) of title 5, United States Code, is amended—
            
                (1)
 in the subsection heading, by striking GS–12 Border Patrol agents and inserting Border Patrol agents classified from grade GS–12 through GS–15; and
            
            
                (2)
 in paragraph (1), by striking a position at grade GS–12 and inserting a position from grade GS–12 through GS–15.
