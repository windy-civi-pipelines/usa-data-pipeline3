Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 to reauthorize the Comprehensive Opioid Abuse Grant Program of the Department of Justice.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Comprehensive Addiction and Recovery Justice Grant Reauthorization Act.

Section 2:
2.ReauthorizationSection 1001(a)(27) of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (34 U.S.C. 10261(a)(27)) is amended—(1)by striking $103,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2017 and 2018, and; and(2)by striking 2019 through 2023 and inserting 2026 through 2030.
