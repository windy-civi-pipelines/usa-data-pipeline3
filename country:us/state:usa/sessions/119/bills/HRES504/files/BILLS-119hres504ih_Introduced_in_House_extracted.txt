RES. 504IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 11, 2025Mrs. Luna submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on EthicsRESOLUTIONRemoving a certain Member from certain standing committees of the House of Representatives.Whereas Representative LaMonica McIver of New Jersey has been charged in a three-count Federal indictment for forcibly impeding and interfering with Federal officers: Now, therefore, be itThat the following named Member be, and is hereby, removed from the following standing committees of the House of Representatives:Committee on Homeland Security:Mrs. McIver.Committee on Small Business:Mrs. McIver.
