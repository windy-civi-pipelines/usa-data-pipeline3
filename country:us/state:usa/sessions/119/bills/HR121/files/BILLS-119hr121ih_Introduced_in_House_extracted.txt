Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit agencies from issuing vaccine passports, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 3
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the No Vaccine Passports Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on agencies issuing vaccine passports(a)In generalAn agency may not issue a vaccine passport, vaccine pass, or other standardized documentation for the purpose of certifying the COVID–19 vaccination status of a citizen of the United States to a third party, or otherwise publish or share any COVID–19 vaccination record of a citizen of the United States, or similar health information.(b)Agency definedIn this section, the term agency has the meaning given that term in section 551 of title 5, United States Code.

Section 3:
3.Prohibition on requiring proof of COVID–19 vaccination for Federal accessProof of COVID–19 vaccination shall not be deemed a requirement for access to Federal property or Federal services, or for access to congressional grounds or services.
