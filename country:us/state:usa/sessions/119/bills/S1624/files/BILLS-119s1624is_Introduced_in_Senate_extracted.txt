Title: 
Official Title: To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to establish a floor on the work geographic index for physicians' services furnished in Hawaii.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Protecting Access To Care in Hawaii Act or the PATCH Act.

Section 2:
2.Floor on work geographic index for physicians' services furnished in HawaiiSection 1848(e) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395w–4(e)) is amended—(1)in paragraph (1)(A), in the matter preceding clause (i), by striking and (I) and inserting (I), and (J); and(2)by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:(J)Floor on work geographic index for services furnished in HawaiiFor purposes of payment for services furnished in Hawaii on or after January 1, 2026, after calculating the work geographic index in subparagraph (A)(iii), the Secretary shall increase the work geographic index to 1.5 if such index would otherwise be less than 1.5. The preceding sentence shall not be applied in a budget neutral manner..
