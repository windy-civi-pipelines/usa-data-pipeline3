Title: 
Official Title: Providing for congressional disapproval of the proposed foreign military sale to the Government of the United Arab Emirates of certain defense articles and services.
Number of Sections: 1
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
That the following proposed foreign military sale to the Government of United Arab Emirates is prohibited: (1)The sale of the following defense articles and services described in Transmittal No. 25–25, submitted to Congress on May 13, 2025, pursuant to section 36(b)(1) of the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2776(b)(1)): F–16 aircraft components, spares, and accessories; and other related elements of logistics and program support.
