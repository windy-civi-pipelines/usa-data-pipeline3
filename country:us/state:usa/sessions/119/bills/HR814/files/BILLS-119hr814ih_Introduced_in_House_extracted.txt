Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 to limit to $0 the amount that the Director of the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection may request to fund the activities of the Bureau.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Defund the CFPB Act.

Section 2:
2.Limiting funding of bureau of consumer financial protectionSection 1017(a) of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 (12 U.S.C. 5497(a)) is amended—(1)in paragraph (1), by striking taking into account and all that follows through the period at the end and inserting which shall be not more than $0.; (2)by striking paragraphs (2) and (3); and(3)by redesignating paragraphs (4) and (5) as paragraphs (2) and (3), respectively.
