IV 
119th CONGRESS 
1st Session 
H. RES. 4 
In the House of Representatives, U. S., 
 
January 3, 2025 
 
RESOLUTION 
 
 
 
That the Clerk be instructed to inform the President of the United States that the House of Representatives has elected Mike Johnson, a Representative from the State of Louisiana as Speaker, and Kevin McCumber, a citizen of the State of Illinois as Clerk, of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Nineteenth Congress.   Kevin F. McCumber,Clerk.
