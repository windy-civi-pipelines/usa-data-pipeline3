Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit the Environmental Protection Agency from using assessments generated by the Integrated Risk Information System as a tier 1 data source in rulemakings and other regulatory, enforcement, or permitting actions, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the No IRIS Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on use of assessments generated by the Integrated Risk Information System programNotwithstanding any other provision of law, the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency may not use any assessment generated by the Integrated Risk Information System program of the Environmental Protection Agency as a basis—(1)to develop, finalize, or issue a rule or regulation; (2)to carry out any other regulatory, enforcement, or permitting action; or(3)to inform air toxics assessments or other mapping or screening tools.
