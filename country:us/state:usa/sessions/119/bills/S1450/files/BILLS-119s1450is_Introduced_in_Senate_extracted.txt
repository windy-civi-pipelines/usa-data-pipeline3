Section 1:
1.Repeal of Open Meetings Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2025The Open Meetings Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 (D.C. Act 26–41) is repealed, and any provision of law amended or repealed by that Act shall be restored or revived as if that Act had not been enacted into law.
