Title: 
Official Title: To amend section 236(c) of the Immigration and Nationality Act with respect to the detention of aliens who commit certain property crimes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Safeguarding American Property Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Detention of certain criminal aliens who commit certain property crimesSection 236(c) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1226(c)) is amended—(1)in paragraph (1)(E)(ii), by inserting trespassing, vandalism, arson, before burglary; and (2)in paragraph (2), by striking and serious bodily injury and inserting serious bodily injury, trespassing, vandalism, and arson.
