        A BILL
        To repeal the authority under the National Labor Relations Act for States to
            enact laws prohibiting agreements requiring membership in a labor organization as a
            condition of employment, and for other purposes.
    
    
        
            1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Nationwide Right To Unionize Act.
        
        
            2.
            Preempting State right-to-work laws
 Subsection (b) of section 14 of the National Labor Relations Act (29 U.S.C. 164) is repealed.
