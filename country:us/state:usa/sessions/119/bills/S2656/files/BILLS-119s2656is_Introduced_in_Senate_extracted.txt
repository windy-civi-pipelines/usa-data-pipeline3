Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Small Business Act to codify the Rule of Two, and for other
            purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Protecting Small Business Competitions Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.
            Codification of the Rule of Two
 Section 15(j) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 644(j)) is amended by adding at the end the following:
            
                
                    (4)
                    Rule of Two
 Each contract, task order, or delivery order for the purchase of goods or services that has an anticipated value greater than the simplified acquisition threshold shall be reserved for small business concerns when the contracting officer reasonably expects—
                    
                        (A)
 to obtain offers from 2 or more responsible small business concerns; and
                    
                    
                        (B)
 to award the contract, task order, or delivery order at a fair market price.
                    
                
                .
