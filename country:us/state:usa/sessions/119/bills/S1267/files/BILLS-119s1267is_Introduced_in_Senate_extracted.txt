Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 38, United States Code, to authorize the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to pay costs associated with the delivery of automobiles adapted for operation by a disabled individual to eligible persons, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Deliver for Veterans Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Authority of the Secretary of Veterans Affairs to pay costs associated with delivery of automobiles or other conveyances to eligible personsSection 3902(a) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by inserting and the total shipping price to deliver the automobile or other conveyance to the eligible person after the total purchase price of the automobile or other conveyance.
