Title: 
Official Title: To require the publication of a detailed justification with respect to certain tariff modifications, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Reviewing Economic and Protection Objectives for Reciprocal Tariffs Act or the REPORT Act.

Section 2:
2.Report required 
(a)Public noticeNot later than 48 hours before the entry into effect of any increase or decrease in a duty pursuant to a provision of law or regulation authorizing modifications on an emergency or discretionary basis to duties imposed on articles imported into the United States, the President shall publish in the Federal Register— (1)notice of such determination; and 
(2)a detailed justification for such increase or decrease. (b)BriefingNot later than 7 days after the date on which the President makes a determination to modify a duty pursuant to an authority described in subsection (a), the United States Trade Representative shall brief the Committee on Ways and Means of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Finance of the Senate on such determination, including the justification for such determination.
