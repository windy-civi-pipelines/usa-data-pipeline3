Title: 
Official Title: To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to remove the 96-hour physician certification requirement for inpatient critical access hospital services.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Critical Access Hospital Relief Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Removing Medicare 96-hour physician certification requirement for inpatient critical access hospital services(a)In generalSection 1814(a) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395f(a)) is amended—(1)in paragraph (6), by adding and at the end;(2)in paragraph (7), at the end of subparagraph (E), by striking ; and and inserting a period; and(3)by striking paragraph (8).(b)ApplicationThe amendments made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to items and services furnished on or after January 1, 2026.
