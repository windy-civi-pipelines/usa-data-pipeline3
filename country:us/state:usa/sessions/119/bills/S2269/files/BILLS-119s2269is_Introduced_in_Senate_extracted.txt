Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 18, United States Code, to increase the criminal penalties for assaulting, resisting, or impeding an officer or employee of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Protect and Respect ICE Act or the PRICE Act.

Section 2:
2.Increased penalties for assaulting, resisting, or impeding ICE officers or employeesSection 111 of title 18, United States Code, is amended—(1)by redesignating subsection (c) as subsection (d); and(2)by inserting after subsection (b) the following:(c)Enhanced penalty for violations against ICE officers or employeesIn the case of a violation of subsection (a) committed against an officer or employee of U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement, the applicable maximum term of imprisonment under this section shall be doubled, and the applicable maximum fine shall be adjusted accordingly..
