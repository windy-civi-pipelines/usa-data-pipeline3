Title: 
Official Title: To recognize Somaliland of the Federal Republic of Somalia as a separate, independent country.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Republic of Somaliland Independence Act.

Section 2:
2.Statement of policy; authorization(a)Statement of policyIt is the policy of the United States that all territorial claims by the Federal Republic of Somalia over the area known as Somaliland are invalid and without merit.(b)AuthorizationThe President is authorized to recognize Somaliland of the Federal Republic of Somalia as a separate, independent country, in accordance with subsection (a).
