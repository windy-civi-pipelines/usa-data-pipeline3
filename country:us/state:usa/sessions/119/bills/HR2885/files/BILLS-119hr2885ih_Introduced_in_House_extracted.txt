Title: 
Official Title: To require the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection to issue a rule before deleting or modifying certain small business loan data, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Bank Loan Privacy Act.

Section 2:
2.Rulemaking requirementSection 704B(e)(4) of the Equal Credit Opportunity Act (15 U.S.C. 1691c–2(e)(4)) is amended—
(1)by striking The Bureau may, and inserting:  (A)In generalThe Bureau may,; and
(2)by adding at the end the following:  (B)Rulemaking requirementThe Bureau shall, before deleting or modifying data under this paragraph issue, through advance notice and comment, a rule that includes a description of what modifications and deletions the Bureau intends to make to the data and how such modifications and deletions will advance a privacy interest. .
