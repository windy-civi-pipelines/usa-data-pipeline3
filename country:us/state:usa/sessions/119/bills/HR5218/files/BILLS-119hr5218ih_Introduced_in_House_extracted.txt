Title: 
Official Title: To direct the Secretary of Homeland Security establish a publicly avaiable web page on the website of the Department of Homeland Security to permit individuals to view active border wall construction and progress, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Border Wall Status Act.

Section 2:
2.Website to view active border wall construction and progressThe Secretary of Homeland Security shall take such actions as may be necessary to establish a publicly available web page on the website of the Department of Homeland Security to permit individuals to view active border wall construction and progress.
