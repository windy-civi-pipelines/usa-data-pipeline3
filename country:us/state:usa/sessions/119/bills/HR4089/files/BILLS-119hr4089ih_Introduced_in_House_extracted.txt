Section 1:
1.Chuck Brown Post Office 
(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3401 12th Street Northeast in Washington, District of Columbia, shall be known and designated as the Chuck Brown Post Office. (b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Chuck Brown Post Office.
