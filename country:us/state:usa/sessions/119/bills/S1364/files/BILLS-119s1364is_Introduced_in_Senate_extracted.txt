Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Supporting American Allies Act.

Section 2:
2.Exemption of articles from Israel and Ukraine from Executive Order imposing reciprocal tariffsThe duties imposed under the Executive Order entitled Regulating Imports with a Reciprocal Tariff to Rectify Trade Practices that Contribute to Large and Persistent Annual United States Goods Trade Deficits shall not apply to any article imported from—(1)Israel; or(2)Ukraine.
