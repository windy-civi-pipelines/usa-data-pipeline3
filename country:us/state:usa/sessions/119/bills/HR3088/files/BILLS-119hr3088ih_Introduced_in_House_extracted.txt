Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 38, United States Code, to include licensed hearing aid specialists under the Veterans Community Care Program of the Department of Veterans Affairs, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Veterans Hearing Health Expansion Act.

Section 2:
2.Inclusion of licensed hearing aid specialists in Veterans Community Care Program of the Department of Veterans AffairsSection 1703(c) of title 38, United States Code, is amended by— (1)redesignating paragraphs (2) through (5) as paragraphs (3) through (6), respectively; and 
(2)by inserting after paragraph (1) the following new paragraph:  (2)A hearing aid specialist licensed to furnish medical services in the State in which such hearing aid specialist is located..
