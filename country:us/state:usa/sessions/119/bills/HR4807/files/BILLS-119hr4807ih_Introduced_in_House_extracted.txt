R. 4807IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJuly 29, 2025Mr. Landsman (for himself, Mr. Ruiz, Ms. Kelly of Illinois, and Mrs. Dingell) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Energy and CommerceA BILLTo amend Public Law 119–21 to repeal certain changes to provider taxes under the Medicaid program. 
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Protect Our Hospitals Act. 
2.Repealing changes to Medicaid provider taxesSection 71115 of Public Law 119–21 is repealed, and the provisions of law amended by such section are restored as if such section had not been enacted into law.
