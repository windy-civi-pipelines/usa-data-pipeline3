Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 37, United States Code, to lower the threshold required for the Secretary of Defense to make a temporary adjustment to a rate of the basic allowance for housing for members of the uniformed services, and to make permanent the authority to make such adjustment.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Service Member Housing Relief Act.

Section 2:
1.Temporary adjustment to a rate of the basic allowance for housing for members of the uniformed services: lower threshold; permanent authoritySection 403(b)(8) of title 37, United States Code, is amended—(1)in subparagraph (A), by striking 20 percent and inserting 15 percent; and(2)by striking subparagraph (C).
