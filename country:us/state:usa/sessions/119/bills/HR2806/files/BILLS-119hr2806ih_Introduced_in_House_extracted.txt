Title: 
Official Title: To prohibit the Secretary of Energy from sending petroleum products from the Strategic Petroleum Reserve to China, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Protecting America’s Strategic Petroleum Reserve from China Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on sales of petroleum products from the Strategic Petroleum Reserve to ChinaNotwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary of Energy shall not draw down and sell petroleum products from the Strategic Petroleum Reserve— 
(1)to any entity that is under the ownership, control, or influence of the Chinese Communist Party; or (2)except on the condition that such petroleum products will not be exported to the People’s Republic of China.
