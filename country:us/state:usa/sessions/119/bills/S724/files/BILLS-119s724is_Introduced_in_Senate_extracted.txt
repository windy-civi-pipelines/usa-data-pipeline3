Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Temporary Extension of Fentanyl-Related Substances Scheduling Act.

2.Extension of temporary order for fentanyl-related substancesSection 2 of the Temporary Reauthorization and Study of the Emergency Scheduling of Fentanyl Analogues Act (Public Law 116–114; 134 Stat. 103) is amended by striking March 31, 2025 and inserting September 30, 2025.
