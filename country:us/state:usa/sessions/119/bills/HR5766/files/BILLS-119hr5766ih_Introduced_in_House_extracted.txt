Title: 
Official Title: To require the Secretary of State to annually award an honorary Trump Peace Prize.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the President Donald J. Trump Peace Prize Act.

Section 2:
2.Trump Peace Prize 
(a)In generalThe Secretary of State shall annually confer an honorary award, to be known as the Trump Peace Prize, on an individual who is a peacemaker. (b)First awardeeThe Secretary shall confer the first Trump Peace Prize on President Donald Trump.
