Official Title: To repeal the District of Columbia Home Rule Act.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Bringing Oversight to Washington and Safety to Every Resident Act or the BOWSER Act.

Section 2:
2.Repeal of District of Columbia Home Rule ActEffective on the date that is 1 year after the date of enactment of this Act, the District of Columbia Home Rule Act (Public Law 93–198; 87 Stat. 774) is repealed.
