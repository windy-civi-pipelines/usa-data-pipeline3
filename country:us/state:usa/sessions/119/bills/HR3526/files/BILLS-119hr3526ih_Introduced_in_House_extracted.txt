Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to increase the limitation on qualified first-time homebuyer distributions, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Uplifting First-Time Homebuyers Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Increasing the limitation on qualified first-time homebuyer distributions (a)In generalSection 72(t)(8)(B)(i) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking $10,000 and inserting $50,000. 
(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by this Act shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2024.
