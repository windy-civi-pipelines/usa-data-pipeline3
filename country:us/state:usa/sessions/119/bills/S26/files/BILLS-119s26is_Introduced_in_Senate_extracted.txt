Section 1:
1.Exclusion of locality adjustments from retirement annuitiesSection 8401 of title 5, United States Code, is amended—(1)in paragraph (3), by inserting , which for a revised average pay employee shall exclude any locality-based comparability payments under section 5304 or 5304a, after rates of basic pay; (2)in paragraph (38)(B), by striking and at the end; (3)in paragraph (39), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and; and(4)by adding at the end the following:(40)the term revised average pay employee means an individual who—(A)on the date of enactment of this paragraph—(i)is not an employee or Member covered under this chapter;(ii)is not performing civilian service which is creditable service under section 8411; and(iii)has not performed any civilian service which is creditable service under section 8411; and(B)after the date of enactment of this paragraph, becomes employed as an employee or becomes a Member covered under this chapter performing service which is creditable service under section 8411..
