1.Assistance for groundwater recharge, aquifer storage, and water source substitution projectsSection 40910 of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (Public Law 117–58) is amended by adding at the end the following:(g)Transfer AuthorityOf the unobligated balances of amounts made available under section 40901, there may be made available to the Secretary $3,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2031 to carry out subsection (a)..
