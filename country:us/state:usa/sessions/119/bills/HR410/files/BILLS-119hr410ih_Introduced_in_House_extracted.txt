Title: 
Official Title: To extend the Alaska Native Vietnam era veterans land allotment program, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Alaska Native Vietnam Era Veterans Land Allotment Extension Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Extension of the Alaska Native Vietnam Era Veterans Land Allotment ProgramSection 1119(b)(3)(B) of the John D. Dingell, Jr. Conservation, Management, and Recreation Act (43 U.S.C. 1629g–1(b)(3)(B)) is amended by striking 5-year period and inserting 10-year period.
