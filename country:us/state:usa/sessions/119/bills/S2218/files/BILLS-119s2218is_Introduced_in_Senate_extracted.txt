Title: 
Official Title: To provide a combat status identifier equivalent for remotely piloted
            aircraft crew who conduct combat operations.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Combat Action Recognition and Evaluation (CARE) for Remotely Piloted Aircraft (RPA) Crews Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.
            Combat status identifier equivalent for remotely piloted aircraft crew
 Not later than 180 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretaries of the military departments shall establish a status identifier of equivalent merit as a combat status identifier for remotely piloted aircraft (RPA) crew who conduct combat operations.
