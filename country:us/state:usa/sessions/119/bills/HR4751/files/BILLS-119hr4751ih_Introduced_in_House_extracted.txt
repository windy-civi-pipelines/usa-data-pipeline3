1.Reauthorization of certain programs 
(a)Swimming pool safety grant programSection 1405(e) of the Virginia Graeme Baker Pool and Spa Safety Act (15 U.S.C. 8004(e)) is amended by striking for fiscal year 2023 and inserting for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2027. (b)Education and awareness programSection 1407(b) of the Virginia Graeme Baker Pool and Spa Safety Act (15 U.S.C. 8006(b)) is amended by striking for fiscal year 2023 and inserting for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2027.
