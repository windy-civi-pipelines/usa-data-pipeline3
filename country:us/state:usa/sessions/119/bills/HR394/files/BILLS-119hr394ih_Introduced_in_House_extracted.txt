Title: 
Official Title: To increase penalties for child pornography.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Holding Child Predators Accountable Act.

Section 2:
2.Possession of child porn(a)ObscenitySection 1466A of title 18, United States Code, is amended—(1)in subsection (a), by striking shall be subject to the penalties provided in section 2252A(b)(1), including the penalties provided for cases involving a prior conviction and inserting shall be fined and punished by death or imprisoned for life; and (2)in subsection (b), by striking shall be subject to the penalties provided in section 2252A(b)(2), including the penalties provided for cases involving a prior conviction and inserting shall be fined and punished by death or imprisoned for life.(b)Exploitation materialSubsection (b) of section 2252 of title 18, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:(b)Whoever violates, or attempts or conspires to violate paragraph (1), (2), (3), or (4) of subsection (a) shall be fined and punished by death or imprisoned for life..(c)Pornography materialSubsection (b) of section 2252A of title 18, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:(b)Whoever violates, or attempts or conspires to violate paragraph (1), (2), (3), (4), (5), (6), or (7) of subsection (a) shall be fined and punished by death or imprisoned for life..
