Title: 
Official Title: To amend title XIX of the Social Security Act to extend renewal periods for certain home and community-based services waivers and State plan amendments under the Medicaid program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Medicaid Empowerment Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Extending renewal periods for certain home and community-based services waivers and State plan amendments under MedicaidSection 1915 of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1396n) is amended— 
(1)in subsection (c)(3), by inserting (or, with respect to extensions of waivers beginning on or after the date of the enactment of the Medicaid Empowerment Act of 2025, 10-year periods) after five-year periods; (2)in subsection (h)(2), by inserting (or, with respect to extensions of waivers under subsection (c) beginning on or after the date of the enactment of the Medicaid Empowerment Act of 2025, 10-year periods) after 5-year periods; and 
(3)in subsection (i)(7)(C), by inserting (or, with respect to renewals of elections beginning on or after the date of the enactment of the Medicaid Empowerment Act of 2025, 10-year terms) after 5-year terms.
