Title: 
Official Title: To allow additional individuals to enroll in standalone dental plans offered through Federal Exchanges.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Increasing Access to Dental Insurance Act.

Section 2:
2.Standalone dental plansSection 1321 of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (42 U.S.C. 18041) is amended by adding at the end the following: 

(f)Availability of standalone dental plansThe Secretary may not restrict any qualified individual from enrolling in a plan described in section 1311(d)(2)(B)(ii) offered through an Exchange established pursuant to subsection (c) on the basis of such qualified individual not being also enrolled in a qualified health plan offered through the Exchange..
