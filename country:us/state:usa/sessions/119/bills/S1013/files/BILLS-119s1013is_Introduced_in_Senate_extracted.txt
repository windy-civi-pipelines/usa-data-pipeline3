Title: 
Official Title: To require the headquarters of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration to be transferred to Brevard County, Florida.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Consolidating Aerospace Programs Efficiently at Canaveral Act or  CAPE Canaveral Act.

Section 2:
2.Transfer of National Aeronautics and Space Administration headquarters to Brevard County, FloridaNot later than 1 year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the headquarters of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration shall be transferred to Brevard County, Florida.
