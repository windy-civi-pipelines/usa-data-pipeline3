Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Securities Act of 1933 to expand the research report exception to include reports about any issuer that undertakes a proposed offering of public securities.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Securities Research Modernization Act.

Section 2:
2.Provision of researchSection 2(a)(3) of the Securities Act of 1933 (15 U.S.C. 77b(a)(3)) is amended— (a)by striking an emerging growth company and inserting an issuer; 
(b)by striking the common equity and inserting any; and (c)by striking such emerging growth company and inserting such issuer.
