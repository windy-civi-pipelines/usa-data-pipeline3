Title: 
Official Title: To ensure due process protections of individuals in the United States against unlawful detention based solely on a protected characteristic.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Korematsu-Takai Civil Liberties Protection Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition against unlawful detentionSection 4001 of title 18, United States Code, is amended—(1)by redesignating subsection (b) as subsection (c); and(2)by inserting after subsection (a) the following:(b)Prohibition on detention based on protected characteristics(1)DefinitionIn this subsection, the term protected characteristic includes each of the following:(A)Race.(B)Ethnicity.(C)National origin.(D)Religion.(E)Sex.(F)Gender identity.(G)Sexual orientation.(H)Disability.(I)Any additional characteristic that the Attorney General determines to be a protected characteristic.(2)ProhibitionNo individual may be imprisoned or otherwise detained based solely on an actual or perceived protected characteristic of the individual. (3)Rule of constructionNothing in this subsection shall be construed to allow the Attorney General to remove a characteristic described in subparagraphs (A) through (H) of paragraph (1)..
