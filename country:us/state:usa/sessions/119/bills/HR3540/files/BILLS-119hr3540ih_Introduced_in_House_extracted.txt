Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to sunset the low-income housing tax credit, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Low-Income Housing Tax Credit Elimination Act.

Section 2:
2.Sunset of low-income housing tax creditSection 42 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:
 
(o)SunsetNo credit shall be determined under subsection (a) with respect to any building placed in service in a taxable year beginning after the date of the enactment of this subsection..
