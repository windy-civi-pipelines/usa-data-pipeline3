Section 1:
1.
            Single, unique ZIP Code for Swanzey, New Hampshire
 Not later than 180 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code applicable to the area encompassing only Swanzey, New Hampshire, in its entirety.
