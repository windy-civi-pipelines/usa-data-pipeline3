Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.ProhibitionNo Federal funds may be obligated or expended by any Federal department or agency for the following: (1)Disinformation research grants. 
(2)Secure and Trustworthy Cyberspace grants. (3)Programs within the National Science Foundation’s Track F: Trust and Authenticity in Communications Systems.
