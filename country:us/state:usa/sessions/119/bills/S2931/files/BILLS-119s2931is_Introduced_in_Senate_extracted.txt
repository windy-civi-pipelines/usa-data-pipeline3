Title: 
Official Title: To reauthorize the National Flood Insurance Program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.
            Short title
 This Act may be cited as the NFIP Extension Act.

Section 2:
2.
            Reauthorization
            
                (a)
                Financing
 Section 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4016(a)) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2026.
            
            
                (b)
                Program expiration
 Section 1319 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4026) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2026.
            
            
                (c)
                Retroactive effective date
 If this Act is enacted after September 30, 2025, the amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall take effect as if enacted on September 30, 2025.
