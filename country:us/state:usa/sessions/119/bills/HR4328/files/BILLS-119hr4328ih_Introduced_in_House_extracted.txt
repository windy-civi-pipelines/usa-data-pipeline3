Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Act of July 10, 1890, to modify certain provisions relating to the disposal of public land in the State of Wyoming for educational purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Wyoming Education Trust Modernization Act.

Section 2:
2.Disposal of public land in the State of Wyoming for educational purposesThe Act of July 10, 1890 (26 Stat. 222, chapter 664), is amended— 
(1)in section 5, in the first sentence, by striking interest of and inserting earnings on; (2)in section 7, by striking interest of and inserting earnings on; and 
(3)in section 8, in the first sentence, by striking income thereof and inserting earnings on which.
