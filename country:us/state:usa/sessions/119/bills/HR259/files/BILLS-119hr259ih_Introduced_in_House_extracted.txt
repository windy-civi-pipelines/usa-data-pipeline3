Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Homeland Security Act of 2002 to prohibit certain advertising with respect to the Immigration Detention Ombudsman, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the No Funding for Illegal Migrant Billboards Act.

Section 2:
2.Prohibition on certain advertising with respect to the Immigration Detention OmbudsmanSection 405 of the Homeland Security Act of 2002 (6 U.S.C. 205) is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection:(f)AdvertisementThe Secretary may not obligate or expend any funds to advertise to the general public, by billboard or otherwise, the office or functions of the Ombudsman..
