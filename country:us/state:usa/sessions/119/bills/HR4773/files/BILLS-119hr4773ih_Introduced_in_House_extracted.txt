Title: 
Official Title: To amend title XVIII of the Social Security Act to establish a SNF-at-home program under the Medicare program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the ACO Assignment Improvement Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Improvements to the assignment of beneficiaries under the medicare shared savings programSection 1899(c)(1) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395jjj(c)(1)) is amended—(1)in subparagraph (A), by striking and at the end;(2)in subparagraph (B), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and; and(3)by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:(C)in the case of performance years beginning on or after January 1, 2027, primary care services provided under this title by an ACO professional described in subsection (h)(1)(B).
