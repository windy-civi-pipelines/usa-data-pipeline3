Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Defund Planned Parenthood Act.

Section 2:
2.ProhibitionNotwithstanding any other provision of law, no Federal funds may be made available to Planned Parenthood Federation of America, or to any of its affiliates.
