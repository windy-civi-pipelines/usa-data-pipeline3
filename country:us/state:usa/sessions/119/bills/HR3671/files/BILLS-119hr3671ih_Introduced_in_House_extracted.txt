Section 1:
1.DesignationThe Federal building located at 300 West Congress Street in Tucson, Arizona, shall be known and designated as the Raúl M. Grijalva Federal Building.

Section 2:
2.ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the Federal building referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to be a reference to the Raúl M. Grijalva Federal Building.
