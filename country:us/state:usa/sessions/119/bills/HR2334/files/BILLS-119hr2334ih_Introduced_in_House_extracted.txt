Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act to preempt any squatter’s rights established by State law regarding real property owned by a member of the uniformed services.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Servicemembers Civil Relief Act: preemption of squatters’ rights(a)PreemptionThe Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (54 Stat. 1178; 50 U.S.C. 3901 et seq.) is amended by inserting, after section 301, the following new section:301A.Preemption of squatter’s rightsAny State law establishing squatter’s rights shall not apply to premises—(1)owned by a servicemember; and(2)occupied by a squatter during a period of military service of such servicemember..(b)Clerical amendmentThe table of contents in section 1(b) of such Act (50 U.S.C. 3901(b)) is amended by inserting after the item relating to section 301 the following new item: 301A. Preemption of squatter’s rights..

Section 2:
301A.Preemption of squatter’s rightsAny State law establishing squatter’s rights shall not apply to premises—(1)owned by a servicemember; and(2)occupied by a squatter during a period of military service of such servicemember.
