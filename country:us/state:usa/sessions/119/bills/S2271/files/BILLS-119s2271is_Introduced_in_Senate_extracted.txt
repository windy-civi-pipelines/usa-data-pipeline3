Section 1:
1.Report on security cooperation with GuyanaNot later than 270 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Defense shall submit to the Committees on Armed Services of the Senate and the House of Representatives a report that describes—(1)the state of United States security cooperation with Guyana; and(2)the additional assistance from the United States necessary to help deter a potential attack by Venezuela on Guyana.
