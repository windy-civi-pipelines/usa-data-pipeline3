Title: 
Official Title: To provide that the Commissioner of Labor Statistics may be removed only with proof of inefficiency, neglect of duty, or malfeasance in office.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Facts First Act.

Section 2:
2.Limitation on removal of Commissioner of Labor StatisticsSection 2 of the Act of June 13, 1888, entitled An act to establish a department of Labor (25 Stat. 182; 29 U.S.C. 3) is amended by adding at the end the following: The Commissioner may be removed only with proof of inefficiency, neglect of duty, or malfeasance in office..
