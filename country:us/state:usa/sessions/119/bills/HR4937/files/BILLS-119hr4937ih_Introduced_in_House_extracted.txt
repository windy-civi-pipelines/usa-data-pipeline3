Title: 
Official Title: To codify the Presidential Memorandum of January 12, 2017, relating to promoting diversity and inclusion in our national parks, national forests, and other public lands and waters.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Expanding Quality and Universal Access to Lands and Parks Act or EQUAL Parks Act.

Section 2:
2.Codifying Presidential Memorandum on promoting diversity and inclusion in public lands and watersThe Presidential Memorandum of January 12, 2017 (Promoting Diversity and Inclusion in Our National Parks, National Forests, and Other Public Lands and Waters; 82 Fed. Reg. 6179) shall have the force and effect of law.
