Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Justice for Exonerees Act.

Section 2:
2.Penalty for unjust conviction and imprisonmentSection 2513 of title 28, United States Code, is amended—
(1)in subsection (e), by striking $50,000 and inserting $70,000; and (2)by adding at the end the following:

(f)The amount of damages awarded under subsection (e) shall be adjusted for inflation annually, based on the Consumer Price Index..
