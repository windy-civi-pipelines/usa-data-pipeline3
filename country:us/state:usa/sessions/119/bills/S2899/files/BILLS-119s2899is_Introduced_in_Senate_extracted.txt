Title: 
Official Title: To authorize additional appropriations to Amtrak for long-distance routes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Rail Service Continuity and Stability Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Additional appropriations to Amtrak for long-distance routesThere are authorized to be appropriated to Amtrak such additional sums as may be necessary to support service for long-distance routes (as that term is defined in section 24102 of title 49, United States Code) existing on the date of the enactment of this Act.
