Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act to modify the Safe Streets and Roads for All program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Safe Streets for All Reauthorization and Improvement Act.

Section 2:
2.Safe streets and roads for all program modificationSection 24112 of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (23 U.S.C. 402 note) is amended— 
(1)in subsection (c)(2) by striking subparagraph (B) and inserting the following:  (B)Planning grantsOf the total amounts made available to carry out the program for fiscal year 2024 and each fiscal year thereafter, not less than 20 percent shall be awarded to eligible projects described in subsection (a)(3)(A).; and 
(2)in subsection (f)(1) by inserting and $5,000,000,000 for the period of fiscal years 2027 through 2031 after 2026.
