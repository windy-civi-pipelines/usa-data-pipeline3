Section 1:
1.Modification to demonstration and prototyping program to advance international product support capabilities in a contested logistics environmentSection 842 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2024 (Public Law 118–31; 10 U.S.C. 2341 note) is amended— (1)in subsection (b)(2)—
(A)in subparagraph (A), by striking and at the end; (B)by redesignating subparagraph (B) as subparagraph (C); and
(C)by inserting after subparagraph (A) the following new subparagraph:  (B)commercial additive manufacturing facilities for rapid, distributed production of parts closer to the point of use; and; and
(2)in subsection (g), by striking on the date and all that follows and inserting December 31, 2030..
