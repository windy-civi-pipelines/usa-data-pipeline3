Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Federal Reserve Act to remove the mandate on the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System and the Federal Open Market Committee to focus on maximum employment.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Price Stability Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Removal of dual mandateSection 2A of the Federal Reserve Act (12 U.S.C. 225a) is amended by striking maximum employment, stable prices, and inserting stable prices.
