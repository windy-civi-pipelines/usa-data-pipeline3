Title: 
Official Title: To permit the televising of Supreme Court proceedings.
Number of Sections: 3
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Cameras in the Courtroom Act.

Section 2:
2.Amendment to title 28 (a)In generalChapter 45 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 
 
678.Televising Supreme Court proceedingsThe Supreme Court shall permit television coverage of all open sessions of the Court unless the Court decides, by a vote of the majority of justices, that allowing such coverage in a particular case would constitute a violation of the due process rights of one or more of the parties before the Court.. (b)Clerical amendmentThe chapter analysis for chapter 45 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 
 
 
678. Televising Supreme Court proceedings..
