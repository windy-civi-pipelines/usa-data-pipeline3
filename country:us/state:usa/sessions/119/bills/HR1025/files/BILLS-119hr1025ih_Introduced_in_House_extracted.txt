Section 1:
1.Cooper Dawson Post Office Building(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 2600 Wesley Street in Greenville, Texas, shall be known and designated as the Cooper Dawson Post Office Building.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Cooper Dawson Post Office Building.
