Title: 
Official Title: To increase the Federal share of the Patrick Leahy Bulletproof Vest Partnership Grant Program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Bipartisan Bulletproof Vest Partnership Program Expansion Act.

Section 2:
2.Increase in grant shareSection 2501 of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 (34 U.S.C. 10531) is amended— 
(1)in subsection (f)(1)— (A)in subparagraph (A), by striking 50 percent and inserting 60 percent; and 
(B)in subparagraph (B), by striking 50 percent and inserting 60 percent; and (2)by striking subsection (h) and inserting the following: 
 
(h)Authorization of appropriationsThere is authorized to be appropriated $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030 to carry out this part..
