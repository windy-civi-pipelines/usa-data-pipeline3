Section 1:
That the following proposed foreign military sale to Israel is prohibited:(1)The sale of the following defense articles and services, described in Transmittal No. 24–16, submitted to Congress pursuant to section 36(b)(1) of the Arms Export Control Act (22 U.S.C. 2776(b)(1)), and published in the Congressional Record on February 10, 2025: an additional 10,000 155mm ancillaries (fuzes, primers, and charges) that will be added to previously implemented cases.
