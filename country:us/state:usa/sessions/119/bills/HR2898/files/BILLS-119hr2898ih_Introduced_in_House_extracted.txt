Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to establish a permanent lookback provision with respect to the Earned Income Credit.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the EITC Lookback Act.

Section 2:
2.Earned income credit lookback(a)In generalSection 32 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new subsection: (o)LookbackIf the earned income of the taxpayer for the taxable year is less than the earned income of the taxpayer for the preceding taxable year, the credit allowed under subsection (a) may, at the election of the taxpayer, be determined by substituting—(1)such earned income for the preceding taxable year, for (2)such earned income for the taxable year. .(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by this section shall apply to determinations made with respect to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2024.
