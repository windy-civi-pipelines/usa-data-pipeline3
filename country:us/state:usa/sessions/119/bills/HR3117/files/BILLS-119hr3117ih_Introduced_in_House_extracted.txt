Section 1:
1. Short title This Act may be cited as the Fairness for Victims of SNAP Skimming Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2. Replacement of stolen EBT benefits Section 501(b)(2) of division HH of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023 (7 U.S.C. 2016a(b)(2)) is amended— 
 (1) by striking subparagraph (A) and inserting the following:   (A) shall be equal to the amount of benefits stolen from the household; and  ;   
 (2) in subparagraph (B), by striking and at the end; and   (3) by striking subparagraph (C).
