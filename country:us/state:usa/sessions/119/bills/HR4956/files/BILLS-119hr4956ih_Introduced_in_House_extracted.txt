Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 40, United States Code, to require a majority of the members of the Commission of Fine Arts to be residents of the District of Columbia.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Commission of Fine Arts District of Columbia Residency Act.

Section 2:
2.Requiring majority of members of Commission of Fine Arts to be District of Columbia residents (a)RequirementSection 9101(b) of title 40, United States Code, is amended by striking appointed by the President, who serve and inserting a majority of whom are residents of the District of Columbia, who shall be appointed by the President to serve. 
(b)Effective dateThe amendment made by subsection (a) shall take effect upon the expiration of the 1-year period which begins on the date of the enactment of this Act.
