Section 1:
1.
      Prohibiting Federal funding for entities that employ individuals who condone and
        celebrate political violence and domestic terrorism
 No Federal funds may be obligated, expended, or otherwise disbursed to any entity that employ individuals who condone and celebrate political violence and domestic terrorism (as that term is defined in section 2331 of title 18, United States Code).
