Title: 
Official Title: To transfer the headquarters of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration to Brevard County, Florida, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Consolidating Aerospace Programs Efficiently at Canaveral Act or the CAPE Canaveral Act.

Section 2:
2.Transfer of National Aeronautics and Space Administration headquarters to Brevard County, FloridaNot later than one year after the date of the enactment of this Act, the headquarters of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration shall be transferred to Brevard County, Florida.
