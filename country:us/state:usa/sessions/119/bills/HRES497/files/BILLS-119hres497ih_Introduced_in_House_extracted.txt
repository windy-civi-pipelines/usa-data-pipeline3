RES. 497IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVESJune 10, 2025Mr. Rulli submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Oversight and Government ReformRESOLUTIONExpressing support for the designation of a Veterans Appreciation Month.Whereas the veterans of the United States have shown steadfast dedication, defending the Nation’s sovereignty, values, and freedoms;Whereas these brave men and women have served with distinction in conflicts across the globe, enduring hardships on land, sea, and air to protect the American way of life;Whereas the sacrifices of veterans, including separation from loved ones, physical and psychological burdens, and the ultimate risk of life, have preserved the liberties that define our great Nation;Whereas June, nestled between Memorial Day’s tribute to our fallen heroes and Independence Day’s celebration of our Nation’s birth, and marked by the historic D-Day anniversary, offers a time to take pride in our veterans’ contributions to this great Nation; andWhereas the people of the United States are proud of the courageous service of all who selflessly served in the United States Armed Forces: Now, therefore, be itThat the House of Representatives supports the designation of a Veterans Appreciation Month.
