Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to repeal the excise tax on indoor tanning services.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Tanning Tax Repeal Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Repeal of excise tax on indoor tanning services (a)In generalSubtitle D of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by striking chapter 49 and by striking the item relating to such chapter in the table of chapters of such subtitle.
(b)Effective dateThe amendments made by this section shall apply to services performed after the date of the enactment of this Act.
