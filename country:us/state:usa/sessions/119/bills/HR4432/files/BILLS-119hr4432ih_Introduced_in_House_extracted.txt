Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Water Resources Development Act of 1992 with respect to the authorized use of certain user fees collected for recreation sites, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short title
 This Act may be cited as the Lanier Parks Local Access Act.

Section 2:
2.Modification to the challenge cost-sharing program for the management of recreation facilitiesSection 225(c)(2)(A)(ii) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1992 (33 U.S.C. 2328(c)(2)(A)(ii)) is amended by striking at recreation site at which the fee is collected and inserting at any recreation site or facility that is located at the civil works project at which the fee is collected.
