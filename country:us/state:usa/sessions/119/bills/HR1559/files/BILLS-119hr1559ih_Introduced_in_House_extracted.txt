Title: 
Official Title: To extend the right of appeal to the Merit Systems Protection Board to certain employees of the United States Postal Service.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

Section 1:
1.Short title This Act may be cited as the Postal Employee Appeal Rights Amendment Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Right of appeal to Merit Systems Protection BoardSection 1005(a)(4)(A)(ii)(I) of title 39, United States Code, is amended to read as follows:

(I)is an officer or employee of the Postal Service who— (aa)is not represented by a bargaining representative recognized under section 1203; and
(bb)is in a supervisory, professional, technical, clerical, administrative, or managerial position covered by the Executive and Administrative Schedule; and.
