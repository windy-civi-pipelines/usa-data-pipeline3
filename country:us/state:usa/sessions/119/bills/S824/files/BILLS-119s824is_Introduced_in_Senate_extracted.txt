Title: 
Official Title: To reauthorize the National Flood Insurance Program.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the NFIP Extension Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Reauthorization(a)FinancingSection 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4016(a)) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2025.(b)Program expirationSection 1319 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4026) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2025.(c)Retroactive effective dateIf this Act is enacted after March 14, 2025, the amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall take effect as if enacted on March 14, 2025.

II119th CONGRESS1st SessionS. 824IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATESMarch 4, 2025Mr. Kennedy (for himself and Mr. Cassidy) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban AffairsA BILLTo reauthorize the National Flood Insurance Program.1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the NFIP Extension Act of 2025.2.Reauthorization(a)FinancingSection 1309(a) of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4016(a)) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2025.(b)Program expirationSection 1319 of the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4026) is amended by striking September 30, 2023 and inserting September 30, 2025.(c)Retroactive effective dateIf this Act is enacted after March 14, 2025, the amendments made by subsections (a) and (b) shall take effect as if enacted on March 14, 2025.
