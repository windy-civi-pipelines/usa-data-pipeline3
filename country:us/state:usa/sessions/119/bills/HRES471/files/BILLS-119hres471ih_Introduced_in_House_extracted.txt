Section 1:
1.Short titleThis resolution may be cited as the Restoring Integrity in Democracy Resolution.

Section 2:
2.Prohibiting Members of the House of Representatives from serving on boards of for-profit entitiesRule XXIII of the Rules of the House of Representatives is amended— 
(1)by redesignating clauses 19 through 22 as clauses 20 through 23, respectively; and (2)by inserting after clause 18 the following new clause: 
 
19.A Member, Delegate, or Resident Commissioner may not serve on the board of directors of any for-profit entity..
