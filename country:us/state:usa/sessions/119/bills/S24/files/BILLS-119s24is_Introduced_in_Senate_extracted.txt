Section 1:
1.Representative Maxine Bell Post Office(a)DesignationThe facility of the United States Postal Service located at 154 First Avenue East in Jerome, Idaho, shall be known and designated as the Representative Maxine Bell Post Office.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Representative Maxine Bell Post Office.
