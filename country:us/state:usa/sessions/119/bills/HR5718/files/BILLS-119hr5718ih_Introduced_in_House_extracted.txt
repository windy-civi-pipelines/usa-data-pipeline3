Title: 
Official Title: To reinstate exemptions to SNAP work-requirements for homeless individuals, veterans, and former foster youth.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the SNAP Back Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Amendment to the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008Section 6(o) of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008 (7 U.S.C. 2015(o)) is amended— 
(1)in subparagraph (F) by striking or at the end, (2)in subparagraph (G) by striking the period at the end, and 
(3)by adding at the end the following:  (H)a homeless individual; 
(I)a veteran; or (J)an individual who is 24 years of age or younger and who was in foster care under the responsibility of a State on the date of attaining 18 years of age or such higher age as the State has elected under section 475(8)(B)(iii) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 675(8)(B)(iii))..
