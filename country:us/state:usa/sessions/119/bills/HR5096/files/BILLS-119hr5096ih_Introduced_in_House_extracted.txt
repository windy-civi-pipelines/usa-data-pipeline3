Title: 
Official Title: To amend title 28, United States Code, to prohibit the exclusion of individuals from service on a Federal jury on account of disability.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Disability and Age in Jury Service Nondiscrimination Act.

Section 2:
2.Exclusion from Federal juries on account of disability
(a)Discrimination prohibitedSection 1862 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by inserting disability, age, after origin,.
(b)Qualifications for jury serviceSection 1865 of title 28, United States Code, is amended—
(1)in subsection (b)(4), by striking infirmity inserting disability that cannot be reasonably accommodated; and
(2)by adding at the end the following:

(c)No person may be disqualified from serving on grand and petit juries in the district court under paragraph (2) or (3) of subsection (b) on account of disability if the person would be qualified under such paragraph (2) or (3) by reasonable accommodation..
