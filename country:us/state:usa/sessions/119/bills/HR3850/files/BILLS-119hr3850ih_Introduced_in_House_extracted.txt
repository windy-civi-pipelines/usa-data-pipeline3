Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Religious Freedom Restoration Act of 1993 to eliminate the applicability of such Act to the District of Columbia.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the District of Columbia Non-Discrimination Home Rule Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Elimination of applicability of Religious Freedom Restoration Act to the District of ColumbiaParagraph (2) of section 5 of the Religious Freedom Restoration Act of 1993 (42 U.S.C. 2000bb–2(2)) is amended by striking the District of Columbia, and the comma after Puerto Rico.
