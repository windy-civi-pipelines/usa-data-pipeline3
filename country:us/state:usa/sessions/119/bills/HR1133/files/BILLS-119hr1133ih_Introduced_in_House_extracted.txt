Title: 
Official Title: To amend the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974 to abolish certain grants for community development, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Repeal Community Development Block Grants Act of 2025.

Section 2:
2.Abolition of grants (a)Abolition of grantsSection 101 and sections 103 through 122 of title I of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974 (42 U.S.C. 5301 et seq.) are repealed. 
(b)Effective dateThe amendments made by this section shall take effect on October 1, 2025.
