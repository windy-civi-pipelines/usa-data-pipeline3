Title: 
Official Title: To redesignate the Hulls Cove Visitor Center at Acadia National Park as the George J. Mitchell Visitor Center.
Number of Sections: 1
Source: versions - Introduced in Senate
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Redesignation of the Hulls Cove Visitor Center at Acadia National Park as the George J. Mitchell Visitor Center(a)RedesignationThe Hulls Cove Visitor Center at Acadia National Park located in Bar Harbor, Maine, or any successor to that visitor center that is constructed after the date of enactment of this Act and serves as the primary visitor center for Acadia National Park, shall be known and designated as the George J. Mitchell Visitor Center.(b)ReferencesAny reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the visitor center referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the George J. Mitchell Visitor Center.
