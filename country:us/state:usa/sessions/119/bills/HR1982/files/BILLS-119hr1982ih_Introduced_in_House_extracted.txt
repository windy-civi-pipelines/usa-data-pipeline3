Title: 
Official Title: To repeal and rescind any unobligated balances under sections 70002 and 70003 of the Inflation Reduction Act, and for other purposes.
Number of Sections: 2
Source: versions - Introduced in House
Media Type: text/xml

================================================================================

Section 1:
1.Short titleThis Act may be cited as the Return to Sender Act.

Section 2:
2.Repeal and rescission of fundsThe unobligated balances of amounts appropriated or otherwise made available by sections 70002 and 70003 of Public Law 117–169 (commonly referred to as the Inflation Reduction Act), as of the date of enactment of this Act are rescinded and those sections are hereby repealed.
